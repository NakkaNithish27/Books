Let me give you clarity on this decision in one minute.

మనం X go చేస్తున్నాం, Y కాదు — that's the call.
We're going with X, not Y — that's the call.

ఈ decision behind reasoning చిన్నగా share చేస్తాను.
Let me briefly share the reasoning behind this decision.

మూడు options consider చేశాం, ఈ one ఎందుకు pick అయ్యిందో చెప్తాను.
We considered three options — let me tell you why this one was picked.

ఎవరెవరు consulted అయ్యారో transparent గా చెప్తాను.
Let me be transparent about who was consulted.

ఈ decision effective from Monday, no phased rollout.
This decision is effective from Monday, with no phased rollout.

Let me assign owners for the next steps.

The decision is made, but feedback on execution is welcome.

ఈ topic మీద decision closed, execution open.

ఈ memo లో decision, rationale, next steps — అన్నీ ఉంటాయి.
The memo will have the decision, the rationale, and the next steps.

"ఎందుకు నన్ను consult చేయలేదు" అని ఎవరికైనా feel అయితే, నేను explain చేస్తాను.
If anyone feels "why wasn't I consulted", I'll walk you through it.

Let's follow the disagree-and-commit principle.

ఈ alternative ఎందుకు reject అయ్యిందో short గా చెప్తాను.
Let me briefly explain why the alternative was rejected.

I'll open the mic, but please don't relitigate the decision.

ఈ decision ఇవ్వాళ్టి meeting tail end announce చేస్తాను.
I'll announce this decision at the tail end of today's meeting.

ఈ decision context ఇచ్చిన తర్వాత, మీకు makes sense అవ్వచ్చు.

ఈ memo subject line — "Decision: <topic>" — easy గా reference చేయొచ్చు తర్వాత.
The memo subject will be "Decision: <topic>" — easy to reference later.

Whoever pushes back, I'll respond with the reasoning.

Before sending this to the team, it was pre-aligned with leadership.

ఈ decision unanimous కాదు అని open గా చెప్తున్నాను.

From now, this decision is the line — let's not have parallel conversations.

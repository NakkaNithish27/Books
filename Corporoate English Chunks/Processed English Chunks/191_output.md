# 191. Strategic planning

ఇవ్వాళ్టి meeting execution కోసం కాదు, strategic step back కోసం.
Today's meeting isn't about execution, it's about a strategic step back.

Next 12 months లో మనం ఏం win చేయాలో, దాని మీద focus.
Let's focus on what we want to win at over the next 12 months.

10 priorities అంటే no priorities, pick 3.
Ten priorities means no priorities — pick three.

ఏం చేయము అని కూడా clear గా write చేద్దాం ఇవ్వాళ.
Let's also clearly write down what we won't do today.

ఈ plan execution proof కాదు, hypothesis గా treat చేద్దాం.
Let's treat this plan as a hypothesis, not as execution-proof.

Baseline ఏంటి, aspiration ఏంటి — రెండూ separate గా చూద్దాం.
Let's separate the baseline from the aspiration.

Sequencing matters, all in parallel చేయగలమా అని reality check చేద్దాం.
Sequencing matters, let's reality-check whether we can run all of this in parallel.

Let's be honest about the resource constraint, otherwise the plan is just fiction.

If there's a pet project, this is the right moment to kill it.

ఈ bet మీద confidence ఎంత ఉంది, percentage లో చెప్పండి.
What's your confidence on this bet — give me a percentage.

OKR ambitious గా ఉండాలి, but believable గా కూడా ఉండాలి.
OKRs should be ambitious, but also believable.

Top-down direction తో bottoms-up reality reconcile చేయాలి.
We have to reconcile top-down direction with bottoms-up reality.

ఈ plan broader org strategy తో align అవుతోందా verify చేద్దాం.
Let's verify this plan aligns with the broader org strategy.

ఈ assumption fail అయితే, plan ఏం అవుతుంది?
If this assumption fails, what happens to the plan?

End of year కి success ఎలా look అవ్వాలో picture లో draw చేయండి.
Paint me a picture of what success looks like at end of year.

ఈ bet high risk, high reward — appetite ఉందా మనకి?
This bet is high-risk, high-reward — do we have the appetite?

Cross-team dependencies ఉన్నాయి, వాటిని early lock చేద్దాం.
The cross-team dependencies — let's lock them down early.

Capacity ఎక్కువ commit చేయొద్దు, buffer keep చేయాలి.
Let's not overcommit on capacity, we need to keep some buffer.

ఈ plan ని leadership కి narrative గా ఎలా sell చేస్తాం?
How do we sell this plan to leadership as a narrative?

ఈ assumption మీద ఒక్క minute stress test చేద్దాం.
Let's stress test this assumption for a minute.

ఈ topic ఇవ్వాళ decide అవ్వదు, parking lot లో పెడదాం.
This won't get decided today, let's put it in the parking lot.

Discussion items వేరు, decision items వేరు — clarify చేసుకుందాం.
Discussion items are one thing, decision items are another — let's clarify which is which.

Over-engineer చేయొద్దు plan ని, simple గా ఉండాలి execute చేయాలంటే.
Don't over-engineer the plan — it needs to be simple to execute.

Pre-mortem ఒక్క minute చేద్దాం — ఈ plan ఎందుకు fail అవ్వొచ్చు?
Let's do a quick pre-mortem — why might this plan fail?

ఈ quarter quick wins ఏం ఉన్నాయో list చేద్దాం.
Let's list out the quick wins for this quarter.

ఈ planning cycle లో last cycle learnings carry forward చేద్దాం, mistakes repeat చేయొద్దు.
Let's carry forward learnings from the last cycle, we shouldn't repeat mistakes.

Trade-offs are unavoidable — there's no room for everything.

ఈ plan iterate అవుతుంది quarter to quarter, not set in stone.
This plan will iterate quarter to quarter, it's not set in stone.

By end of session, we should walk away with a one-page narrative and three priorities.

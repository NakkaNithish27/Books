# 281. Pitching ideas

Naaki ఒక idea undi, share cheddam.
I've got an idea, let me share.
---
Two minutes ivvandi, ee pitch cover chesta.
Give me two minutes, I'll cover this pitch.
---
Quick ga ఒక thought, mee feedback teesukondam.
A quick thought, let me get your feedback.
---
Naaki ee problem konchem time nundi observe chestunna.
I've been observing this problem for a while.
---
Ee gap ni address cheyali ane idea undi.
I have an idea to address this gap.
---
Ee solution try cheste ela undi ane question undi naaku.
I'm asking — what if we try this solution?
---
Problem ee, solution ee — ade ground lo cheppa.
Here's the problem, here's the solution — let me lay it out.
---
"What if" tho start cheddam.
Let's start with "what if".
---
Idi simple ga undi kani impact pedda untundi.
This is simple but has big impact.
---
30-second pitch ela undi ante...
The 30-second pitch is like...
---
Naa hypothesis ee, validate cheyali.
Here's my hypothesis, need to validate it.
---
One-line ga cheppali ee idea ni.
Need to say this idea in one line.
---
Elevator pitch ela undi ante...
The elevator pitch goes like...
---
Ee idea ki ఒక sponsor undali leadership lo.
This idea needs a sponsor in leadership.
---
Mee daggara nundi 15 minutes ki time kavali pitch ki.
I'd need 15 minutes of your time for the pitch.
---
Slide deck ready ga undi, share chestha.
The slide deck is ready, I'll share.
---
Quick demo kuda chupiyestha.
I'll also show a quick demo.
---
Visual ga chuste better land avtundi.
It lands better visually.
---
Numbers tho start chesi pitch lokela vellali.
Start with numbers, then go into the pitch.
---
"Ee market 5 billion size" antam ముందు.
Say "this market is 5 billion size" upfront.
---
Hook strong ga undali first 10 seconds lo.
The hook needs to be strong in the first 10 seconds.
---
Vaaru ki "tell me more" anipiyali.
They should feel "tell me more".
---
Idea ni small bites lo break cheddam.
Let's break the idea into small bites.
---
Big leap kāadu, incremental ga cheppali.
Not a big leap, say it incrementally.
---
Pilot tho start chesi scale cheddam ane angle.
Angle of starting with a pilot and scaling.
---
"Low cost, high learning" ga frame cheddam.
Frame it as "low cost, high learning".
---
Vaaru ki risk perceive avvakuda show cheyali.
Should show it without making them perceive risk.
---
"Failure scenario lo em pothundi" ade ముందు cheppali.
Need to say upfront "what's the loss in failure scenario".
---
Naaki ee idea ki bandwidth, $5K budget kavali.
For this idea I need bandwidth and a $5K budget.
---
Small ask, big upside — ade naa angle.
Small ask, big upside — that's my angle.
---
Yes ante manam start chestam ee week.
If you say yes, we'll start this week.
---
2 weeks lo prototype ready, 4 weeks lo data.
Prototype in 2 weeks, data in 4 weeks.
---
Naaki ee pitch confidence tho ivvali, hesitation kāakunda.
I need to give this pitch with confidence, no hesitation.
---
Eye contact, body language matter avtundi pitch lo.
Eye contact, body language matter in the pitch.
---
Q&A ki ready ga undali, top 5 questions anticipate cheyali.
Ready for Q&A, need to anticipate the top 5 questions.
---
"Idi enduku scale avtundi" ade question ki strong answer.
Need a strong answer to "why will this scale".
---
"Competitor ela handle chestunnaru" ade question kuda vasthundi.
The question "how are competitors handling this" will also come.
---
Close strong ga undali — call to action tho.
The close needs to be strong — with a call to action.
---
"Naaki yes or no ee week lo telustunda?" ade ask.
"Can I get a yes or no this week?" — that's the ask.
---
Pitch tarvata follow-up email ee EOD pampedham.
After the pitch, we'll send a follow-up email by EOD.
---
Vaaru busy ayna ee idea memory lo undali.
Even if they're busy, this idea needs to stay in their memory.
---
Final ga ee pitch ni manager tho dry run cheddam.
Finally let's dry-run this pitch with the manager.

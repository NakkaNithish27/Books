# 200. Executive influence

ఈ exec కి ఆయన language లో pitch చేయాలి, మన language లో కాదు.
We need to pitch this to the exec in their language, not ours.

ఈ ask ఆయన KPIs తో directly tie చేస్తే, half the battle is won.
If we tie this ask directly to their KPIs, half the battle is won.

ఈ exec revenue care చేస్తారు, frame కి revenue narrative add చేయి.
This exec cares about revenue, frame the narrative around revenue.

Pre-meeting 1:1 పెట్టుకుందాం, room లో surprise అవ్వకుండా.
Let's set up a pre-meeting 1:1, so they're not surprised in the room.

ఈ idea ఆయన ఆ idea గా feel అయితే, adoption faster.
If they feel like it's their idea, adoption is faster.

ఈ exec కి concise గా pitch — 60 seconds, 3 bullets, 1 ask.
Pitch this exec concisely — 60 seconds, three bullets, one ask.

ఈ ask మీద ఇతర peer execs already supportive గా ఉన్నారని signal ఇవ్వు.
Signal that other peer execs are already supportive of this ask.

ఈ exec కి chief of staff ద్వారా influence చేస్తే, sometimes faster.
Sometimes going through the chief of staff influences the exec faster.

ఈ exec deck simple గా ఉండాలి, ఎక్కువ data exec కి distract.
The exec deck has to be simple — too much data distracts an exec.

ఈ customer story ఆయన attention ని lock చేస్తుంది, anchor as opener.
That customer story will lock their attention, anchor on it as the opener.

ఈ meeting లో coalition already pre-aligned అయితే, decision smooth.
If the coalition is pre-aligned before the meeting, the decision is smooth.

ఈ ask room లో directly పెట్టే ముందు, 3 individual 1:1s build చేస్తాను.
Before putting this ask in the room, I'll build it through three individual 1:1s.

ఈ exec కి seed plant చేస్తే, 2-3 weeks లో own చేస్తారు.
Plant the seed with this exec, in 2–3 weeks they'll own it.

ఈ idea ఆయన mouth నుండి repeat అవ్వడం our goal.
Our goal is to have them repeat the idea in their own words.

ఈ pre-read ఒక్క page ఉండాలి, exec కి time లేదు.
The pre-read should be one page, execs don't have time.

ఈ ask social proof తో strong అవుతుంది — "Mike already aligned" అని add చేయి.
Add social proof — say "Mike is already aligned" — it strengthens the ask.

ఈ exec కి risk-averse default mindset, risk-mitigation angle highlight చేయి.
This exec defaults to risk-averse, highlight the risk-mitigation angle.

ఈ meeting outcome ఏం expect చేస్తున్నావో upfront చెప్పు.
State upfront what outcome you're expecting from this meeting.

ఈ exec influence build అవ్వాలంటే, ఆయనకి value add చేయాలి regularly.
To build influence with this exec, you need to add value to them regularly.

ఈ exec time precious, walking-in clarity కావాలి, fishing కాదు.
Their time is precious — walk in with clarity, not to fish.

ఈ meeting ని non-decision meeting గా frame చేస్తే, less defensive గా listen చేస్తారు.
If you frame it as a non-decision meeting, they'll listen less defensively.

ఈ exec కి their boss already endorsed అని signal ఇవ్వడం sometimes okay.
Sometimes signalling that their boss has already endorsed it is okay.

ఈ topic మీద ఆయన hesitation ఎక్కడ ఉందో decode చేయాలి.
We need to decode where their hesitation on this topic is coming from.

ఈ exec నుండి verbal yes గా walk out అవ్వాలి, polite ambiguity కాదు.
We need to walk out with a verbal yes, not polite ambiguity.

Post-meeting recap email send చేస్తాను, commitments lock చేస్తుంది.
I'll send a post-meeting recap email — that locks in the commitments.

ఈ exec కి "what's in it for them" angle ముందు చెప్పి, then the ask.
With this exec, lead with "what's in it for them," then come to the ask.

ఈ ask present చేసేటప్పుడు, ఆయన rivals ఎవరో navigate చేయాలి.
When presenting this ask, navigate around who their rivals are.

ఈ exec కి "less is more" rule, ఎక్కువ talk చేస్తే signal దిగుతుంది.
With this exec, "less is more" — the more you talk, the more the signal degrades.

ఈ moment లో push back off చేయడం better, next quarter లో retry చేస్తాను.
In this moment it's better to back off, I'll retry next quarter.

ఈ exec sponsorship అడిగితే, specific గా ఏం sponsorship అని define చేయి.
If you're asking for sponsorship, define specifically what kind of sponsorship.

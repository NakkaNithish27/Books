# 282. Strategic planning

Manam next year plan mida kurchondam.
Let's sit down on next year's plan.
---
Annual planning start ayindi, themes lock cheyali.
Annual planning has started, need to lock the themes.
---
Big bets enti ee year ki - decide cheddam.
What are the big bets for this year — let's decide.
---
3 themes mida focus cheddam, ekuva pettoddu.
Let's focus on 3 themes, not more.
---
Quarter wise breakdown cheddam ee plan ni.
Let's break this plan quarter-wise.
---
Q1 lo foundation, Q2 lo scale, Q3 lo polish.
Q1 foundation, Q2 scale, Q3 polish.
---
North star metric enti ee year ki?
What's the north star metric for this year?
---
Vision statement clear ga undali ముందు.
The vision statement needs to be clear first.
---
Mission ki tie avvali ee initiatives andharu.
All these initiatives need to tie to the mission.
---
"Run the business" vs "change the business" balance cheyali.
Balance "run the business" vs "change the business".
---
70% run, 30% change ratio chaalu naaki.
70% run, 30% change ratio is enough for me.
---
Budget allocation ela undali ee year ki?
How should budget allocation look this year?
---
Headcount ki ఎక్కువ, tooling ki తక్కువ pedadama?
More on headcount, less on tooling?
---
Capacity planning ni real ga cheyali.
We need to do real capacity planning.
---
Each team capacity enta undi map cheyali.
We need to map each team's capacity.
---
Stretch goals vs realistic goals separate cheyali.
Separate stretch goals from realistic goals.
---
Stretch goals motivate chestayi, kani sane ga undali.
Stretch goals motivate, but should stay sane.
---
Scenario planning cheddam — best case, base case, worst case.
Let's do scenario planning — best case, base case, worst case.
---
Worst case lo manam ela respond chestam ade plan undali.
We should have a plan for how we'd respond in worst case.
---
Strategic priorities ni 3-year horizon lo chudali.
Look at strategic priorities in a 3-year horizon.
---
Long term thinking matter avtundi planning lo.
Long-term thinking matters in planning.
---
Tactical vs strategic distinguish cheyali.
Distinguish tactical vs strategic.
---
Em chesthe tactical, em chesthe strategic chudali.
See what's tactical and what's strategic.
---
Bets ni tier-wise rank cheddam — Tier 1, 2, 3.
Let's rank bets tier-wise — Tier 1, 2, 3.
---
Tier 1 ki guaranteed funding, Tier 3 ki contingent.
Tier 1 guaranteed funding, Tier 3 contingent.
---
Manam ఏం say no antunnamo ade plan ni define chesthundi.
What we say no to defines the plan.
---
Saying no to good ideas hard, kani avasaram.
Saying no to good ideas is hard but necessary.
---
Focus ki ee year ekuva avasaram, fragmentation kāadu.
This year needs more focus, not fragmentation.
---
KPIs ni define cheyali success ki.
Define KPIs for success.
---
Each initiative ki ఒక OKR set chesta.
For each initiative, set an OKR.
---
Outcome metrics matter, output metrics kāadu.
Outcome metrics matter, not output metrics.
---
Manam ela measure chestam ade ముందు decide cheyali.
We need to decide how we'll measure first.
---
Mid-year review undali, course correct ki.
Need a mid-year review for course correction.
---
Quarterly business review tho track cheddam.
Let's track via quarterly business reviews.
---
Plan static kāadu, living document.
The plan isn't static — it's a living document.
---
Strategy ki execution ki gap padakuda chudali.
Watch out for gap between strategy and execution.
---
Each leader ki ee plan ki sign off undali.
Each leader needs to sign off on this plan.
---
Top-down vs bottom-up — balance ga rendu use cheddam.
Top-down vs bottom-up — let's use both balanced.
---
Skip-level alignment kuda kavali plan ki.
Need skip-level alignment for the plan too.
---
Final ga ee plan ni andhariki communicate cheddam clearly.
Finally let's clearly communicate this plan to everyone.
---
Pre-mortem cheddam — em fail avvocchu ade chudali ముందు.
Let's do a pre-mortem — see what could fail first.
---
Final draft ki review cycle iddam ee week.
Let's give the final draft a review cycle this week.

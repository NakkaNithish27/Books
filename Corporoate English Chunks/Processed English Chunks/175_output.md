# 175. Sponsorship building

If this senior leader becomes my sponsor, my career will get a boost.

I need to gradually build a relationship with my skip manager.

I'll update my sponsor once this project wraps up.

Mentor and sponsor are different, I need both.

ఈ leader trust build అవ్వడానికి consistent delivery కావాలి.
Consistent delivery is needed to build trust with this leader.

ఈ leader meetings లో నన్ను observe చేస్తున్నారు.

I'll try to set up a casual coffee chat with this leader.

ఈ promotion conversation లో sponsor weight తీసుకొస్తారు.
The sponsor will bring weight in the promotion conversation.

ఈ leader champion గా ఉన్నారు నా side.
This leader is championing my side.

ఈ network leader level లో build చేయాలి.
I need to build this network at the leader level.

Speaking up in front of this leader is key, can't stay silent.

I'll forward this piece of work directly to my sponsor.

I should attend the skip-level breakfast.

I need to maintain this visibility consistently, can't go off the radar.

ఈ leader memory లో నేను rising talent గా stay అవ్వాలి.

Sponsorship doesn't get built overnight, it needs patience.

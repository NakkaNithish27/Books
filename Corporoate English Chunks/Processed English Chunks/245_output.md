# 245. Camera etiquette

Camera on చేయగలరా please?
Can you turn your camera on, please?

అందరూ camera on ఉంచుదాం ఈ call కి.
Let's all keep cameras on for this call.

Just a request, mandatory కాదు.
Just a request, it's not mandatory.

Camera off ఉంటే disengaged గా feel అవుతోంది.
When cameras are off, it feels disengaged.

Today కొంచెం camera off గా ఉండాలి, ఇల్లు background.
I need to keep my camera off today, the background is messy.

Virtual background పెట్టుకోండి అవసరం అయితే.
Use a virtual background if needed.

Lighting కొంచెం తక్కువ ఉంది, sorry.
The lighting is a bit dim, sorry.

Camera quality బాగా ఉంది మీది.
Your camera quality looks great.

కొంచెం pixelated వస్తోంది మీది.
Yours is coming through a bit pixelated.

Bandwidth issue ఉంటే off చేయొచ్చు.
Feel free to turn it off if there's a bandwidth issue.

Important meetings లో camera on ఉండడం better.
For important meetings, it's better to keep cameras on.

Leadership కి face value పెరుగుతుంది.
It adds face value with leadership.

అందరూ off అయితే awkward గా ఉంటుంది moderator కి.
If everyone's off, it feels awkward for the moderator.

కనీసం speaker camera on ఉండాలి.
At least the speaker should have their camera on.

Eating అవుతుంటే mute and camera off చేయండి.
If you're eating, mute and turn camera off.

Background లో family members ఉన్నారు.
There are family members in the background.

అది cover చేయడానికి blur పెట్టుకుంటాను.
I'll use blur to cover that.

కొంచెం professional setup కావాలి office room లాగా.
We need a professional setup, like an office room.

తయారవ్వడానికి 5 minutes ఇవ్వండి.
Give me 5 minutes to get ready.

Camera angle కొంచెం adjust చేయండి.
Adjust your camera angle a bit.

Face full గా visible గా ఉండాలి.
Your full face should be visible.

చాలా close గా ఉన్నారు camera కి.
You're a bit too close to the camera.

కొంచెం దూరం move అవ్వండి.
Move back a bit.

Recording అవుతోంది, professional గా ఉంచుదాం.
It's being recorded, let's keep it professional.

Daily standups లో camera off ok.
Camera off is fine for daily standups.

Client calls లో మాత్రం camera on ఉండాలి.
For client calls though, camera has to be on.

That's basic etiquette in remote work.
That's basic etiquette in remote work.

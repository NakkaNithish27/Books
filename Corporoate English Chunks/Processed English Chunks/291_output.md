# 291. Vision alignment

Andhariki same vision visible undali ముందు.
Everyone needs visibility on the same vision first.
---
Manam ekkadiki vellaalanukuntunnamo ade clear ga undali.
Where we're heading must be clear.
---
Vision ni andhariki cascade cheddam team meeting lo.
Let's cascade the vision to everyone in the team meeting.
---
Just leadership chusedi kāadu, andhariki kavali.
Not just for leadership to see, everyone needs it.
---
Mission, vision, values — andhi connect cheyali.
Connect mission, vision, values.
---
Daily work ki vision ela tie avtundo cheppali.
Need to say how daily work ties to the vision.
---
Vaaru ki "naa work matter avtunda?" ade clear undali.
"Does my work matter?" — that must be clear to them.
---
Each role ki vision ki connection cheppali.
Need to show vision connection for each role.
---
Manam ee daggara ela contribute chestunnam ade visualize cheyali.
Visualize how we're contributing here.
---
"Big picture" ni repeat ga cheppali multiple forums lo.
Repeat the "big picture" in multiple forums.
---
Once cheppadame chālu kāadu, ade reinforce avvali.
Saying it once isn't enough, it needs reinforcement.
---
Town hall lo vision ni revisit cheddam quarterly.
Revisit the vision quarterly in the town hall.
---
Vision statement crisp ga undali, paragraph kāadu.
Vision statement should be crisp, not a paragraph.
---
One sentence lo cheppagaligithe perfect.
If you can say it in one sentence, perfect.
---
Vaaru ki memorize avvali, just hear kāadu.
They need to memorize it, not just hear it.
---
Team interview cheste, vaaru same answer ivvali "where are we going" question ki.
If we interview the team, they should give the same answer to "where are we going".
---
Misalignment vasthe immediate ga reset cheddam.
If misalignment shows up, reset immediately.
---
Quarterly all-hands lo vision check up.
Vision check-up in quarterly all-hands.
---
North star definition mida andharu agree avvali.
Everyone needs to agree on the north star definition.
---
Two-year vision vs ten-year vision separate undali.
Two-year vision vs ten-year vision should stay separate.
---
Near-term vision tactical, long-term vision strategic.
Near-term vision is tactical, long-term is strategic.
---
Vision ki strategy ki gap raakuda chudali.
Watch out for gap between vision and strategy.
---
Each team's vision rolls up to org vision.
Each team's vision rolls up to the org vision.
---
Sub-team mission org mission ki align avvali.
Sub-team mission has to align with the org mission.
---
Manaki cross-team alignment kuda kavali ee vision lo.
We need cross-team alignment on this vision too.
---
Vision ki signs of life undala team lo?
Are there signs of life on the vision in the team?
---
Pulse survey lo "I understand the vision" rating chudali.
Look at "I understand the vision" rating in the pulse survey.
---
Vision uncertain ga undi feedback lo, revisit cheyali.
Feedback shows vision is uncertain — need to revisit.
---
Leadership commitment visible undali vision ki.
Leadership commitment must be visible to the vision.
---
Walk the talk leadership nundi expected.
Walk the talk is expected from leadership.
---
Vision posters okka room lo undi chalavu, communication lo undali.
Vision on posters in a room isn't enough, must be in communication.
---
Every email signature mida vision ela undali small ga.
The vision should be on every email signature in some small form.
---
Stories tho vision ni reinforce cheyali.
Reinforce the vision with stories.
---
Customer success stories ki tie cheddam vision tho.
Tie customer success stories to the vision.
---
"Idi vision ki ela connect avtundi" ade question prati decision ki.
"How does this connect to the vision?" — for every decision.
---
Decision filter ga vision use cheyali daily.
Use the vision as a daily decision filter.
---
Misaligned features ni drop cheddam, focus undali.
Drop misaligned features, focus is needed.
---
Each OKR ki vision link clearly undali.
Each OKR should clearly link to the vision.
---
New joiner ki onboarding lo vision ముందు cheppali.
For new joiners, vision goes first in onboarding.
---
"You're joining for this" — ade clarity day 1 nundi.
"You're joining for this" — that clarity from day 1.
---
Final ga vision ni live ga ఉంచాలి, document mida kāadu.
Finally, the vision needs to stay alive, not just on paper.
---
Manam andharu rowing in same direction undali.
We all need to be rowing in the same direction.

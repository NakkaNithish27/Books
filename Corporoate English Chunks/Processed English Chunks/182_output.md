# 182. Vision communication

మనం ఎక్కడికి వెళ్తున్నామో ఒక్క minute clarity ఇస్తాను.
Let me give you a minute of clarity on where we're headed.

ఈ year end కల్లా మనం ఈ product ని market leader గా చూడాలి.
By the end of this year, we want to see this product as the market leader.

Daily work busy గా ఉంటుంది, but big picture మర్చిపోకూడదు.
Day-to-day work gets busy, but we can't lose sight of the big picture.

మన goal complicated ఏం కాదు — make the customer's life easier.
Our goal isn't complicated — make the customer's life easier.

ఇది just one project కాదు, ఇది ఒక direction.
This isn't just one project, it's a direction.

3 years తర్వాత ఈ team ఎక్కడ ఉంటుందో, that's what we're building toward.
Where this team will be in 3 years — that's what we're building toward.

మనం ఏం చేస్తామో అదే important కాదు, ఎందుకు చేస్తామో అది ముఖ్యం.
It's not just what we do, it's why we do it that matters.

Short term లో pain ఉంటుంది, but long term win is huge.
There'll be short-term pain, but the long-term win is huge.

ఈ vision realistic, but ambitious — that's the point.
This vision is realistic but ambitious — that's the point.

మనం ఇక్కడ market follower గా ఉండడానికి రాలేదు.
We're not here to be a market follower.

Every quarter మనం ఈ direction లో ఒక step move అవుతున్నామా అని check చేసుకోవాలి.
Every quarter we need to check if we're moving one step in this direction.

ఇది clear గా ఉండాలి అందరికీ — priorities are these three, nothing else.
This needs to be crystal clear to everyone — these three are the priorities, nothing else.

మీ daily work కూడా ఈ vision కి connect అవుతుంది, నేను చూపిస్తాను.
Even your daily work connects to this vision, let me show you how.

Customer ఏం feel అవ్వాలి అనేది మన North Star.
What the customer feels is our North Star.

ఈ direction లో మనం stick అయితే, results follow అవుతాయి.
If we stick to this direction, results will follow.

మనం ఏం చేయము అని కూడా clear గా ఉండాలి.
We also need to be clear about what we will not do.

ఈ vision నేను top down impose చేయట్లేదు, it's a shared direction.
I'm not imposing this vision top down, it's a shared direction.

Some quarters slow గా feel అవుతుంది, but we're compounding.
Some quarters will feel slow, but we're compounding.

మనం build చేస్తున్నది just a product కాదు, a category.
We're not just building a product, we're building a category.

5 years తర్వాత మీ resume లో ఈ work proud గా చెప్పాలి.
Five years from now, you should be proud to put this work on your resume.

ఈ mission లో meaningful గా contribute చేయని work మనం avoid చేద్దాం.
Let's avoid any work that doesn't meaningfully contribute to this mission.

Repeat చేస్తున్నాను అని feel అవ్వొచ్చు, but vision needs to be said again and again.
You might feel I'm repeating myself, but the vision needs to be said again and again.

ఈ direction లో నేను fully committed, మరి మీరు?
I'm fully committed to this direction, what about you?

Doubts ఉంటే open గా ask చేయండి, vision questioned అవ్వడం healthy.
If you have doubts, ask openly — it's healthy for the vision to be questioned.

Roadmap shift అవ్వొచ్చు, but vision stays the same.
The roadmap may shift, but the vision stays the same.

మనకి success ఎలా look అవ్వాలో నేను describe చేస్తాను, then we work backwards.
Let me describe what success looks like, and then we work backwards from there.

Noise చాలా ఉంటుంది outside, మన focus inside ఉండాలి.
There'll be a lot of noise outside, our focus has to stay inside.

ఈ vision నేను రేపు మార్చను, you can count on that.
I'm not going to change this vision tomorrow, you can count on that.

మనం ఎక్కడ ఉన్నామో, ఎక్కడికి వెళ్తున్నామో — ఈ gap ని close చేయడమే మన job.
Where we are versus where we're going — closing that gap is our job.

ఈ vision simple గా చెప్పాలంటే — be the team everyone wants to work with.
If I had to put this vision simply — be the team everyone wants to work with.

Strategy మారొచ్చు, tactics మారొచ్చు, but the destination stays fixed.
Strategy can change, tactics can change, but the destination stays fixed.

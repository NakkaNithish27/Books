# 111. Buy-in conversations

Idi mida mee buy-in undali ముందు.
We need your buy-in on this first.
---
Vaaru full ga on board ena ee approach mida?
Are they fully on board with this approach?
---
Naaki buy-in unte naa team kuda support ivvtundi.
If I have buy-in, my team will support too.
---
Ee plan ki andhariki commit avvali అన్నది important.
The important thing is everyone commits to this plan.
---
Vaaru ki ee thing ela benefit chestundo cheppali ముందు.
We need to tell them how this benefits them first.
---
Concerns unte ippudē share cheyandi.
If there are concerns, share now.
---
Silence ante yes kāadu, explicit ga cheppali.
Silence doesn't mean yes, has to be explicit.
---
Mee thoughts cheppandi - in or out?
Tell me your thoughts — in or out?
---
Ee plan ki yes ante full commitment ani arthamu.
Saying yes to this plan means full commitment.
---
Manaki konchem soft buy-in chālu kāadu, strong buy-in kavali.
A soft buy-in isn't enough, we need a strong buy-in.
---
Mee push back unte ippude ivvandi, tarvata kaadu.
If you have push back, give it now, not later.
---
Vaaru reluctant ga unnaru ila chesthe enduku?
They're reluctant — why is that?
---
Vaaru ఏం block chestunnaaro అది అర్థం avvali.
We need to understand what's holding them back.
---
Naaku enni questions unna answer chestha, vaaru on board avvali.
I'll answer however many questions, they need to be on board.
---
Vaaru ki ownership ivvali, appude vaaru invest avtaru.
We need to give them ownership, only then they'll invest.
---
Idi mana plan kāadu, mana plan ani vaaru feel avvali.
This shouldn't feel like our plan, it should feel like their plan.
---
Co-create cheddam ee solution ni.
Let's co-create this solution.
---
Vaaru ki credit ivvadam mistake kāadu ee daggara.
Giving them credit here isn't a mistake.
---
Naa ki commitment kavali tomorrow lopu.
I need a commitment by tomorrow.
---
Ee thing mida sign off ivvagalara today?
Can you sign off on this today?
---
Verbal commit ivvandi ee call lo, written tarvata.
Give a verbal commit in this call, written can follow.
---
Vaaru ఇంకా middle lo unnaru, decision teesukoleadu.
They're still in the middle, haven't decided.
---
Ee fence mida kurchodam okay kāadu naaku.
Sitting on the fence isn't okay with me.
---
Yes or no - clear ga cheppali.
Yes or no — must be clear.
---
Naa ki ee project ki funding ki buy-in kavali.
I need buy-in for funding on this project.
---
Budget approve cheste manam start cheyagalam.
If the budget is approved, we can start.
---
Vaaru ka resources ki block cheste tarvata problem kāadu.
If they block resources upfront, no problem later.
---
Stakeholder commitment lekapote project struggle avtundi.
Without stakeholder commitment, the project will struggle.
---
Aligned ga ledhu ante deliver cheyalemu honestly.
If we're not aligned, honestly we can't deliver.
---
Mee support telustaru appude push chestha.
I'll push only when I know I have your support.
---
Ee message vaariki naa daggara nundi vinapadudu cheppedi.
This message shouldn't sound like it's only from me.
---
Manam combined ga show cheyali united front.
We need to show a united front together.
---
Vaaru ki ee context ivvali, just plan kāadu.
We need to give them the context, not just the plan.
---
Why ade arthamu chesthe yes vaste vasthundi.
If they understand the why, the yes follows.
---
Trust build cheste vaaru auto ga buy chestaru.
If trust is built, they'll automatically buy in.
---
Idi vaaru ki personal win ela undo cheppali.
We need to tell them how this is a personal win for them.
---
Skeptics ni ఒక chair lo address cheddam first.
Let's address the skeptics first in one go.
---
Vaaru side ki tippisi vesthe migatha vaaru follow avutaru.
If we turn them, the rest will follow.
---
Champion role ki ఎవరిని teesukundam ee thing ki?
Who do we take as the champion for this?
---
Ee proposal ki ఒక internal sponsor undali.
This proposal needs an internal sponsor.
---
Manaki blessing kavali leadership nundi.
We need a blessing from leadership.
---
Final ga andharu mida ye yes set ayye varaku push cheddam.
We'll push until we get a yes from everyone.

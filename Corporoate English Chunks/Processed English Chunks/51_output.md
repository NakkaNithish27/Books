# 51. Saying no professionally

దీన్ని take up చేయలేను.
I won't be able to take this up.

---

నేను ఇప్పుడు కొంచెం stretched గా ఉన్నాను.
I'm a bit stretched right now.

---

నా side నుండి possible కాదు.
Not going to be possible from my end.

---

ఆ timeline కి commit అవ్వలేను.
Won't be able to commit to that timeline.

---

దీన్ని [team] బాగా handle చేయగలదు.
I think this is better handled by [team].

---

ఇది నా scope లో లేదు.
This isn't really in my scope.

---

దీన్ని next week కి push చేయగలమా?
Could we push this to next week?

---

Meeting attend అవ్వలేను.
Won't be able to attend the meeting.

---

ఈ ఒక్కటి నేను skip చేయాలి.
I'll have to skip this one.

---

Sorry, ఇప్పుడు దీన్ని take on చేయలేను.
Sorry, can't take this on right now.

---

ఈ week నాకు bandwidth లేదు.
Bandwidth is full for the week.

---

దీన్ని prioritize చేయలేను.
Won't be able to prioritize this.

---

[name] తో check చేయగలరా? వారు better placed.
Could you check with [name]? They'd be better placed.

---

Help చేయడానికి ready, కానీ ఈ week కాదు.
Happy to help, but not this week.

---

మొదట priorities re-look చేయగలమా?
Could we re-look at the priorities first?

---

ఇది wait చేయాలి.
This will need to wait.

---

Manager తో check చేయకుండా commit అవ్వలేను.
Can't commit without checking with my manager.

---

నా side నుండి అది hard no.
That's a hard no from my side.

---

Politely decline చేయాల్సి వస్తోంది.
I'll have to politely decline.

---

నాకు right fit కాదు.
Not the right fit for me.

---

దీన్ని తరువాత take up చేయగలనా?
Could I take this up later?

---

X తో help చేయగలను, కానీ Y తో కాదు.
I can help with X but not Y.

---

దీనిలోకి pull అవ్వకూడదు అని.
I'd rather not get pulled into this.

---

Sorry, other deliverables తో blocked.
Sorry, blocked with other deliverables.

---

ఆ week leave మీద ఉంటాను.
Out on leave that week.

---

ఇప్పటికే double-booked.
Already double-booked.

---

దీన్ని తరువాత revisit చేయగలమా?
Could we revisit this later?

---

దీన్ని take on చేసే position లో లేను.
Not in a position to take this on.

---

ఇది నా area కాదు.
This is outside my area.

---

Sorry, attend చేయలేను.
Sorry, won't be able to make it.

---

దీన్ని drop చేయాలి.
Have to drop this one.

---

దీన్ని ఇప్పటికి park చేద్దాం.
Let's park this for now.

---

Feasible కాదు.
Not going to be feasible.

---

ఈ ask మీద push back చేయాలి.
Will need to push back on this ask.

---

Clarity వచ్చేవరకు commit అవ్వకూడదు.
I'd rather not commit until I have clarity.

---

ఇది existing commitments మీద impact చేస్తుంది.
This will impact existing commitments.

---

ఇది చేయాలంటే, ఇంకొకటి drop అవ్వాలి.
If we do this, something else has to give.

---

దీన్ని next quarter లో చూడగలమా?
Could we look at this next quarter?

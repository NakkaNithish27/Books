295. Strategic prioritization

మనం everything ఒకేసారి చేయలేం.
We can't do everything at once.

ఏది ముందు do చేయాలో decide చేద్దాం.
Let's decide what to do first.

అన్నీ important, but అన్నీ urgent కాదు.
Everything is important, but not everything is urgent.

మనకి clear priorities కావాలి.
We need clear priorities.

ఇది top priority గా treat చేయాలి.
We need to treat this as top priority.

మిగిలినవి later quarter కి push చేద్దాం.
Let's push the rest to a later quarter.

Bandwidth limited, so focus important.
Bandwidth is limited, so focus matters.

ఈ initiative impact ఎక్కువ ఇస్తుంది.
This initiative will give us more impact.

Low-value items ని drop చేయాలి.
We need to drop low-value items.

Top 3 priorities మీద మాత్రమే focus పెడదాం.
Let's focus only on the top 3 priorities.

ఇది must-have నా, nice-to-have నా?
Is this a must-have or a nice-to-have?

ROI based గా prioritize చేద్దాం.
Let's prioritize based on ROI.

Leadership కి top 5 items తో go అవ్వాలి.
We should go to leadership with the top 5 items.

ఏదైనా add చేస్తే, ఏదో ఒకటి drop అవ్వాలి.
If we add something, we have to drop something.

Trade-offs unavoidable.
Trade-offs are unavoidable.

ఇది strategic గా worth చేయదగ్గది ఆ?
Is this strategically worth doing?

Customer impact ని ముందు చూడాలి.
We should look at customer impact first.

Revenue-driving items ముందు రావాలి.
Revenue-driving items should come first.

ఈ quarter focus ఇదే అని clear గా చెప్దాం.
Let's clearly say this is the focus for this quarter.

Distractions ని avoid చేయాలి.
We need to avoid distractions.

Every yes is a no to something else.
Every yes is a no to something else.

Team కి clarity ఇవ్వడం చాలా important.
Giving clarity to the team is very important.

Backlog ని ruthlessly prune చేద్దాం.
Let's ruthlessly prune the backlog.

Strategic alignment తో priorities decide అవ్వాలి.
Priorities should be decided with strategic alignment.

# 107. Executive updates

Leadership ki ఒక short update tayaru cheddam.
Let's prepare a short update for leadership.
---
Vaaళ్ళకి details vaddu, just highlights chālu.
They don't need details, just highlights are enough.
---
Bottom line ni ముందు cheppali.
We need to lead with the bottom line.
---
Top 3 points only, ఎక్కువ pettoddu.
Only top 3 points, don't put more.
---
RAG status ena - Green, Amber, Red?
What's the RAG status — Green, Amber, Red?
---
Status amber lo undi ee week.
The status is amber this week.
---
Red ki vellaledhu inka, kani close.
We haven't gone to red yet, but close.
---
Ee update ni ఒక slide lo fit cheyandi.
Fit this update on one slide.
---
Execs ki long emails pampoddu, summary chālu.
Don't send long emails to execs, a summary is enough.
---
Asks enti, decisions enti - clearly call out cheyandi.
What are the asks, what are the decisions — clearly call out.
---
Action requested from leadership cheppali ముందు.
Mention the action requested from leadership upfront.
---
Risks ni hide cheyakandi, openly cheppandi.
Don't hide the risks, surface them openly.
---
Wins kuda highlight cheyandi.
Highlight the wins too.
---
Numbers ni ముందు pettali, narrative tarvata.
Lead with the numbers, narrative after.
---
Ee deck ki ఒక executive summary slide add cheddam.
Let's add an exec summary slide to this deck.
---
Exec time limited, 5 minutes lo cover cheyali.
Exec time is limited, we need to cover it in 5 minutes.
---
Vaaళ్ళకి technical detail vaddu, business impact చాలు.
They don't need technical detail, business impact is enough.
---
Idi ఎంత revenue impact చేస్తుందో chudaali.
We need to look at how much revenue impact this has.
---
Status: on track, on budget.
Status: on track, on budget.
---
Slightly behind schedule, kani recoverable.
Slightly behind schedule, but recoverable.
---
Two risks unnayi, ee slide lo cover chesa.
There are two risks, I've covered them in this slide.
---
Mitigation plan kuda ready ga undali.
Mitigation plan should also be ready.
---
Vaaru deep ga dive avvataru, ready ga undali.
They may deep dive, we need to be ready.
---
Backup slides petti undali questions ki.
We should keep backup slides for questions.
---
Ee question ki naa daggara answer ledhu, follow up chesta.
I don't have an answer to this question, I'll follow up.
---
Idi follow up item ga note cheddam.
Let's note this as a follow-up item.
---
Trends gurinchi adugutharu, last 3 months data ready ga undali.
They'll ask about trends, keep last 3 months data ready.
---
Comparison with last quarter kuda ivvali.
We should also give a comparison with last quarter.
---
Story line clear ga undali deck lo.
The storyline needs to be clear in the deck.
---
Ee deck ki review feedback ఎవరి daggara nundi తీసుకుందాం?
Whose review feedback do we take on this deck?
---
Manager review chesi sign off ivvali ముందు.
Manager has to review and sign off first.
---
Dry run cheddam ముందు actual call ki.
Let's do a dry run before the actual call.
---
Idi exec attention ki teesukelladu confidently.
Take this to exec attention confidently.
---
Avoid surprises in the exec meeting.
Avoid surprises in the exec meeting.
---
Vaaru ki ముందే heads up ivvali risks gurinchi.
Give them a heads up about risks beforehand.
---
Skip-level ki ee point clearly land avvali.
This point needs to clearly land with the skip-level.
---
One-pager pampedham executive ki, deck kāadu.
Let's send a one-pager to the exec, not a deck.
---
Em decisions kavali vaaru side nundi - clear ga listed undali.
What decisions are needed from their side — should be clearly listed.
---
Idi inform only ena, or decision needed ena?
Is this for information only, or decision needed?
---
Vaaru busy, straight to the point cheppali.
They're busy, we need to go straight to the point.
---
Recap and next steps tho mugincheyali.
We should wrap up with recap and next steps.
---
Send the update by EOD today, please.
Send the update by EOD today, please.

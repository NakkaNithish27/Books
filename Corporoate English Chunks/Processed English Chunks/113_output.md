# 113. Pushback handling

Mee point arthamayindi, naa side kuda cheppedi vinandi.
I understand your point, please hear my side too.
---
Mee concern valid, addressing chesthanu.
Your concern is valid, I'll address it.
---
Konchem unpack cheddam ee point ni, exactly enti concern?
Let's unpack this point — what exactly is the concern?
---
Naaki disagree cheyadam okay, kani reasoning cheppandi.
It's okay to disagree, but please share the reasoning.
---
Idi mida em specifically disagree avtunnaru?
What specifically are you disagreeing on?
---
Naaki feedback kavali, kani actionable ga undali.
I want feedback, but it should be actionable.
---
Ee push back behind reason enti?
What's the reason behind this pushback?
---
Naa logic ee, mee logic enti?
Here's my logic, what's yours?
---
Manam common ground chudali ee mida.
We need to find common ground on this.
---
Naaku open ga undali ee discussion ki.
I need to be open in this discussion.
---
Mee alternative enti? cheppandi.
What's your alternative? Tell me.
---
Just no ante chālu kāadu, why ade cheppali.
Just saying no isn't enough, you need to say why.
---
Naaki ee point pakka clear ga undali ముందు proceed avvadaniki.
This point needs to be crystal clear before I proceed.
---
Naaki idi unfair ga undi anipistundi.
This seems unfair to me.
---
Konchem step back avvali manam ee daggara.
We need to step back a bit here.
---
Naa ki disagree cheyali ee approach mida.
I have to disagree with this approach.
---
Strongly disagree, kani open ga vinatanu.
I strongly disagree, but I'm open to hearing it.
---
Idi naaki not aligned with the goal ani anipistundi.
This doesn't feel aligned with the goal to me.
---
Mee push back ni note chesa, kani naa stance same ne.
I've noted your pushback, but my stance is the same.
---
Manam agree to disagree avvali ee mida.
We need to agree to disagree on this.
---
Ee decision naa pay grade above, escalate cheddam.
This decision is above my pay grade, let's escalate.
---
Manager ki teesukelladu ee debate ni.
Let's take this debate to the manager.
---
Konchem cool down avvi tarvata matladdam.
Let's cool down and talk later.
---
Idi personal kāadu, just professional disagreement.
This isn't personal, just a professional disagreement.
---
Naaki kuda chala options consider chesa, ade settled.
I've considered many options too, this one is settled.
---
Mee perspective ki value ivvtanu, kani decision naa de.
I value your perspective, but the decision is mine.
---
Ee decision naa accountability lo undi, naade teesukuntha.
This decision is under my accountability, I'll take it.
---
Final call naa ki teesukokovali, vintha mee input.
The final call is mine to take, but I hear your input.
---
Naa mind ni change cheyalanukunte data ivvandi.
If you want to change my mind, give me data.
---
Just opinion mida disagree cheste convince kaalemu naaku.
Disagreeing on just opinion won't convince me.
---
Numbers chudali, anubhavam matladali.
We need to look at numbers, experience speaks.
---
Mee daggara better proposal unte share cheyandi.
If you have a better proposal, share it.
---
Lekapote ee plan continue avtundi.
Otherwise this plan continues.
---
Naa boundary ee, dani daggara naaki firm ga undali.
This is my boundary, I need to hold firm there.
---
Ee back and forth endless avtundi, mugincheyali ipudē.
This back and forth is going forever, we need to wrap up.
---
Manam ee call lo decision teesukondam ే.
Let's take a decision in this call itself.
---
Naa side nundi compromise ee daggara possible.
On my side compromise is possible up to this point.
---
Mee side nundi em flex undi cheppandi.
Tell me what flex you have on your side.
---
Win-win chudali, ledhante stalemate.
We need win-win, otherwise it's a stalemate.
---
Ee pushback ni note chesthe document chestha follow up ki.
I'll document this pushback for follow-up.
---
Constructive pushback welcome, blocker ki kāadu.
Constructive pushback is welcome, not blocking.
---
Final ga manam move avvali, eppude perfect agreement ranadu.
Finally we have to move, perfect agreement won't always come.

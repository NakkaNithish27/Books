# 183. Delegating authority

ఈ piece ఇవ్వాళ నుండి నువ్వే own చేయి.
You own this piece from today.

నేను ప్రతి decision లో involve అవ్వను, నీ call.
I won't be involved in every decision, it's your call.

Stuck అయితే మాత్రమే నన్ను loop చేయి, otherwise drive it.
Loop me in only if you're stuck, otherwise just drive it.

ఈ scope లో full authority నీది.
Within this scope, you have full authority.

చిన్న చిన్న things కి నా approval అడగాల్సిన అవసరం లేదు.
You don't need to check with me for the small stuff.

నీ judgment మీద నాకు trust ఉంది, take the call and move.
I trust your judgment, take the call and move on.

Team కి కూడా announce చేస్తాను, నువ్వే ఈ work కి point person.
I'll announce to the team too — you're the point person for this work.

Mistake అయినా okay, నేను back చేస్తాను.
Even if there's a mistake, I've got your back.

ఈ decisions నీ level లోనే close అవ్వాలి, escalate చేయొద్దు.
These decisions should close at your level, don't escalate them up.

నీ way లో solve చేయి, నా way necessarily right ఏం కాదు.
Solve it your way, my way isn't necessarily the right way.

Weekly ఒక్కసారి sync అవుదాం, daily updates అక్కర్లేదు.
Let's sync once a week, I don't need daily updates.

ఈ budget within లో నీ discretion.
Within this budget, it's at your discretion.

Hiring call నీది, నేను just second opinion ఇస్తాను.
The hiring call is yours, I'll just give a second opinion.

Stakeholders తో directly deal చేయి, నన్ను CC పెట్టు.
Deal with the stakeholders directly, just keep me on CC.

Authority ఇస్తున్నాను, accountability కూడా వస్తుంది — that's the deal.
I'm giving you authority, accountability comes with it — that's the deal.

ఈ scope outside ఏదైనా decision ఉంటే అప్పుడు మాత్రం నన్ను check చేయి.
Anything outside this scope, then check with me.

నేను step back అవుతున్నాను, you take the lead here.
I'm stepping back, you take the lead here.

Roadblocks ఉంటే చెప్పు, నేను clear చేస్తాను, but the work is yours.
If there are roadblocks, tell me, I'll clear them — but the work is yours.

ఈ project nuances నీకు better తెలుసు, నీ instinct follow అవ్వు.
You know the nuances of this project better than me, follow your instinct.

Public గా నేను నీ call ని challenge చేయను, concerns ఉంటే 1:1 లో చెప్తాను.
I won't challenge your call in public, if I have concerns I'll raise them 1:1.

Approval needed అని feel అయితే, just go ahead — consider it approved.
If you feel approval is needed, just go ahead — consider it approved.

ఈ ownership నీది అని team కి clear గా తెలియాలి.
The team needs to be clear that this ownership is yours.

నీ name ఈ work తో attach అవ్వాలి, నాది కాదు.
Your name should be attached to this work, not mine.

Outcome కి accountable నువ్వే, but I'm here as a sounding board.
You're accountable for the outcome, but I'm here as a sounding board.

చిన్న చిన్న things over-engineer చేయొద్దు, just make the call.
Don't over-engineer the small things, just make the call.

Postmortem వచ్చినా, నేను నీ side నిలబడతాను.
Even if there's a postmortem, I'll stand by your side.

ఈ team ని manage చేయడం ఇప్పటి నుండి నీ responsibility.
Managing this team is your responsibility from now on.

Comfortable అయితే take it, hesitate అవ్వకు — you're ready.
If you're comfortable, take it, don't hesitate — you're ready.

ఎప్పుడైనా advice కావాలంటే door open, but don't wait for permission.
Whenever you need advice my door's open, but don't wait for permission.

నేను shadow చేస్తాను initially, then completely hand it over.
I'll shadow you initially, then hand it over completely.

ఈ decision నీ name మీద వెళ్తుంది, own it fully.
This decision goes out under your name, own it fully.

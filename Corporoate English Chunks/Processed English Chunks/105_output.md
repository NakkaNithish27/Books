# 105. Requirement gathering

Exactly em kaavalo cheppandi okasari.
Tell me exactly what you need, once.
---
Problem enti, ade konchem detail ga cheppandi.
Tell me a bit more about the problem.
---
Idi ఎవరు use chestaru, end user ఎవరు?
Who'll use this, who's the end user?
---
Current ga ela jaragutundi, ela cheyalanukuntunnaru?
How is it happening today, how do you want it to work?
---
Manaki ఒక example ivvagalara?
Can you give me an example?
---
Use case enti exactly?
What's the use case exactly?
---
Inputs enti, outputs enti?
What are the inputs, what are the outputs?
---
Manam edi solve cheyaali ee thing tho?
What are we solving with this?
---
Idi must-have ena, or good-to-have ena?
Is this a must-have or a good-to-have?
---
Priority enti ee feature ki?
What's the priority for this feature?
---
Existing process enti, naaki adi telustunda?
What's the existing process, can you walk me through it?
---
Konchem ee workflow detail ga walk through cheyandi.
Walk me through this workflow in detail.
---
Pain point enti exactly user side lo?
What's the pain point exactly on the user side?
---
Idi ela use avvali ani anukuntunnaru?
How do you envision this being used?
---
Edge cases lo em jaragutundi?
What happens in edge cases?
---
If user ee data ivvaledhu ante em cheyali?
What should happen if the user doesn't provide this data?
---
Validation rules ఏమైనా unnayaa?
Are there any validation rules?
---
Acceptance criteria enti?
What are the acceptance criteria?
---
Idi pass avvadaniki ఏం unte chālu?
What's needed for this to be considered done?
---
Success ela measure cheddam?
How do we measure success?
---
Volume enta untundi roughly?
What's the rough volume?
---
Frequency ela untundi, daily, weekly?
What's the frequency — daily, weekly?
---
Existing system tho ela integrate avvali?
How should this integrate with the existing system?
---
Dependencies em em unnayi?
What are all the dependencies?
---
Idi ఇంకా ఎవ్వరితో impact avtundi?
Who else does this impact?
---
Ee thing ki stakeholders evaru?
Who are the stakeholders for this?
---
Manaki ఒక sample data ivvagalara?
Can you give me some sample data?
---
Screenshots leda mockups unte share cheyandi.
If you have screenshots or mockups, please share.
---
Ee ask behind reason enti?
What's the reason behind this ask?
---
Why ade అర్థం avvali, what kaadu mathrame.
We need to understand the why, not just the what.
---
Ee field mandatory ena, optional ena?
Is this field mandatory or optional?
---
Default value enti undali?
What should the default value be?
---
Idi ఎవరు approve chestaru workflow lo?
Who approves this in the workflow?
---
Notification em em pampali, evariki?
What notifications should go out, and to whom?
---
Error handling ela undali?
How should error handling work?
---
Reporting ki em em fields kaavali?
What fields are needed for reporting?
---
Idi mobile lo kuda work avvala or web only?
Should this work on mobile too or web only?
---
Permissions ela manage cheddam?
How do we manage permissions?
---
Naaku ee point mida clarity ledhu ఇంకా.
I don't have clarity on this point yet.
---
Idi naaku written form lo ivvagalara please?
Can you give me this in writing, please?
---
Manam ee requirements ni document cheddam ఇప్పుడే.
Let's document these requirements right now.
---
Confirmation ki ee BRD ni okasari review cheyandi.
Please review this BRD once for confirmation.
---
Inka ఏమైనా requirements pending ga unte cheppandi.
If there are any pending requirements, please tell me.

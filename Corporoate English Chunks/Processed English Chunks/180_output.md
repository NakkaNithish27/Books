# 180. Leadership alignment

ఈ direction లో leadership అందరూ aligned అయ్యారా ముందు check చేస్తా.
Let me first check if all of leadership is aligned on this direction.

ఈ message leadership నుండి consistent గా రావాలి, mixed signals కాదు.
The message should come consistently from leadership, not mixed signals.

ఈ leadership offsite లో priorities locked అయ్యాయా?
Were the priorities locked in the leadership offsite?

ఈ strategy రోల్ ఔట్ చేయడానికి ముందు leadership అలైన్ చేయాలి.
Before rolling out this strategy, leadership needs to be aligned.

ఈ disconnect leadership level లో ఉంది, ground level లో solve అవ్వదు.
This disconnect is at the leadership level, won't solve at the ground.

ఈ leaders ఒకే page మీద ఉంటే team execution easy.
If the leaders are on the same page, team execution becomes easy.

ఈ decision behind leadership unanimous గా backing ఇవ్వాలి.
Leadership needs to back this decision unanimously.

ఈ matter లో మీరిద్దరూ ఒకే view లో ఉన్నారా?
Are both of you in the same view on this matter?

ఈ alignment లేకుండా announcement చేయడం risky.
Announcing this without alignment is risky.

ఈ leadership confusion ground కి passdown అవుతోంది, fix చేయాలి.
This leadership confusion is passing down to the ground, needs to be fixed.

ఈ leaders తో pre-call పెట్టుదాం, main meeting ముందు.
Let's set up a pre-call with the leaders before the main meeting.

ఈ priority list లో leadership confirm చేస్తేనే team work మొదలుపెడుతుంది.
Only if leadership confirms this priority list will the team start work.

ఈ messaging leadership team draft చేసి approve చేయాలి.
The leadership team needs to draft and approve this messaging.

ఈ point మీద leaders ఒకరితో ఒకరు align అవ్వాలి ముందు.
The leaders need to align with each other first on this point.

ఈ leadership inconsistency వలన team frustrate అవుతోంది.
The team is getting frustrated because of this leadership inconsistency.

ఈ strategy unified గా present చేయాలి, individual views కాదు.
This strategy needs to be presented unified, not individual views.

ఈ leader ఒక view చెబుతున్నారు, ఆ leader వేరే view, confused గా ఉంది.
This leader is saying one thing, that leader another, it's confusing.

ఈ alignment town hall ముందు lock అవ్వాలి.
This alignment needs to be locked before the town hall.

ఈ leadership decision ఒక voice తో రావాలి.
The leadership decision should come in one voice.

ఈ matter లో united front ఉండాలి leadership నుండి.
There needs to be a united front from leadership on this matter.

ఈ direction shift leadership అందరికీ communicate అయ్యిందా?
Has this direction shift been communicated to all of leadership?

ఈ leadership cascade through manager level జరగాలి.
This leadership cascade needs to flow through manager level.

ఈ ask leadership consensus తోనే close అవ్వాలి.
This ask can close only with leadership consensus.

ఈ priorities re-aligned అవ్వాలి leadership level లో.
The priorities need to get re-aligned at the leadership level.

ఈ strategy business leaders సహాయంతో finalize అవ్వాలి.
This strategy needs to be finalized with help from the business leaders.

ఈ matter leadership sync లో formally raise చేయాలి.
This matter needs to be formally raised in the leadership sync.

ఈ goal cascade అయ్యేటప్పుడు అందరూ ఒకే number మాట్లాడాలి.
When the goal cascades down, everyone should be quoting the same number.

ఈ leadership అన్ని directions ఒకసారే decision ఇవ్వాలి.
Leadership needs to give one decision across all directions.

ఈ alignment లేకపోతే mid-execution change లు వస్తాయి.
Without alignment, mid-execution changes will keep coming.

ఈ topic leadership steering committee discuss చేయాలి.
This topic needs to be discussed in the leadership steering committee.

ఈ message leadership channel ద్వారా reinforce అవ్వాలి.
This message needs to be reinforced through the leadership channel.

ఈ leadership singing from the same hymn sheet కావాలి.
Leadership needs to be singing from the same hymn sheet.

ఈ direction confirm అయ్యాక మాత్రమే execution start అవుతుంది.
Execution will start only after the direction is confirmed.

ఈ leadership review లో alignment formal గా sign-off అవ్వాలి.
This alignment needs to get formally signed-off in the leadership review.

ఈ leaders ఒకరి decision ని ఒకరు publicly support చేయాలి.
The leaders should publicly back each other's decisions.

275. Long-term career planning

5 years తర్వాత నేను ఎక్కడ ఉండాలి అని ఆలోచిస్తున్నాను.
I'm thinking about where I should be 5 years from now.

ఇప్పుడు చేస్తున్న పని long-term లో ఎక్కడికి తీసుకెళ్తుంది?
Where will my current work take me in the long term?

నాకు ఒక clear direction కావాలి.
I need a clear direction.

Just job చేస్తూ ఉంటే career build అవ్వదు.
Just doing the job won't build a career.

నేను ఏ role కి grow అవ్వాలి అన్నది decide చేయాలి.
I need to decide what role I want to grow into.

Manager track కావాలా లేక technical track కావాలా?
Do I want a manager track or a technical track?

ఈ decision early లో తీసుకుంటే better.
It's better to take this decision early.

నాకు ఏ work satisfaction ఇస్తుందో identify చేయాలి.
I need to identify what work gives me satisfaction.

Money మాత్రమే చూస్తే long-term లో burn out అవుతాం.
If I only look at money, I'll burn out in the long term.

Growth, learning, stability - అన్నీ balance చేయాలి.
Growth, learning, stability - I need to balance all of them.

ప్రతి 2 years కి ఒక skill upgrade ఉండాలి.
Every 2 years there should be a skill upgrade.

ఒకే place లో stuck అవ్వకూడదు.
I shouldn't get stuck in one place.

Industry ఎలా change అవుతుందో observe చేస్తుండాలి.
I should keep observing how the industry is changing.

5 years తర్వాత ఈ skill relevant గా ఉంటుందా?
Will this skill be relevant 5 years from now?

AI వల్ల ఏ roles impact అవుతాయో చూస్తున్నాను.
I'm watching which roles will be impacted by AI.

Future-proof skills మీద focus పెట్టాలి.
I need to focus on future-proof skills.

నాకు ఒక mentor ఉంటే path clear గా ఉంటుంది.
If I have a mentor, the path will be clearer.

Already అదే journey done చేసిన వాళ్ళతో మాట్లాడాలి.
I should talk to people who have already done that journey.

వాళ్ళ mistakes నుండి learn అవ్వాలి.
I should learn from their mistakes.

Career అన్నది straight line కాదు.
A career isn't a straight line.

కొన్ని side steps కూడా అవసరం.
Some side steps are also necessary.

Sometimes lateral move కూడా growth.
Sometimes a lateral move is also growth.

Title కంటే experience important.
Experience is more important than title.

ఒక్కో stage లో priorities change అవుతాయి.
Priorities change at each stage.

Early career లో learning, mid career లో impact, later లో legacy.
Early career is learning, mid career is impact, later is legacy.

నేను ఏ stage లో ఉన్నానో అది అర్థం చేసుకోవాలి.
I need to understand what stage I'm in.

Yearly గా career review చేసుకుంటాను.
I do a yearly career review.

Last year compared to this year ఎంత grow అయ్యానో check చేస్తాను.
I check how much I've grown compared to last year.

Just years గడిపాను అంటే అది growth కాదు.
Just spending years doesn't count as growth.

Same work repeat చేస్తే అది 1 year experience 10 times.
Repeating the same work is 1 year of experience 10 times.

నేను ఏ company లో ఎక్కువ grow అవుతానో అదే చూస్తాను.
I look at which company I can grow more in.

Brand name కంటే growth opportunity important.
Growth opportunity is more important than brand name.

Backup plan ఎప్పుడూ ఉండాలి.
There should always be a backup plan.

ఒక job మీద completely depend అవ్వకూడదు.
I shouldn't depend completely on one job.

Side skills, network, savings - అన్నీ build చేసుకోవాలి.
Side skills, network, savings - I need to build all of them.

Family priorities కూడా career planning లో consider చేయాలి.
Family priorities should also be considered in career planning.

Personal life and career balance చేయాలి.
I need to balance personal life and career.

Long-term plan rigid గా ఉండకూడదు, flexible గా ఉండాలి.
The long-term plan shouldn't be rigid, it should be flexible.

Opportunities వచ్చినప్పుడు adapt అవ్వాలి.
I need to adapt when opportunities come.

కానీ direction మాత్రం clear గా ఉండాలి.
But the direction should remain clear.

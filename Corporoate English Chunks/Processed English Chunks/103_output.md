# 103. Alignment discussions

Manam andharu same page lo unnamaa?
Are we all on the same page?
---
Idi mida align avvali ముందు.
We need to align on this first.
---
Ee approach mida andharu agree ena?
Does everyone agree on this approach?
---
Manaki alignment ledhu inka.
We're not aligned yet.
---
Ee point mida ఒకసారి clarity techukundam.
Let's get clarity on this point once.
---
Manam ee call ni alignment ki use cheddam.
Let's use this call for alignment.
---
Andhariki same understanding undala?
Does everyone have the same understanding?
---
Ee meeting goal alignment ni reach avvadame.
The goal of this meeting is to reach alignment.
---
Idi mida andharu sound chesara final ga?
Is everyone sound on this finally?
---
Manam ee decision mida align ayyamu antara?
Can we say we're aligned on this decision?
---
Ee approach ki andharu okay ena?
Is everyone okay with this approach?
---
Naaki idi different ga అర్థం ayindi.
I understood this differently.
---
Manam ఒక common ground ki ravali.
We need to come to a common ground.
---
Ee mida ఇంకా discussion avasaram.
This needs more discussion.
---
Andhariki visibility undala ee decision mida?
Does everyone have visibility on this decision?
---
Naa understanding correct ena?
Is my understanding correct?
---
Ee point mida disagreement undi, openly matladadam better.
There's disagreement on this point, better to discuss openly.
---
Manam ఒకే direction lo unnama or different ga unnama?
Are we going in the same direction or different ones?
---
Ee call ki end ki ఒక decision tisukundam.
By the end of this call, let's take a decision.
---
Andharu input ivvandi, appude alignment vastundi.
Everyone give input, only then we'll get alignment.
---
Silence ante agreement kaadu, openly cheppandi.
Silence doesn't mean agreement, speak up openly.
---
Naaki concern undi ee approach mida, share cheddam.
I have a concern on this approach, let me share.
---
Manam ee call lo close cheddam ee item ni.
Let's close this item in this call.
---
Aksar idi different teams lo different ga implement avtundi, align cheddam.
This is often implemented differently across teams, let's align.
---
Ee decision documented ga undali, andhariki visibility kosam.
This decision needs to be documented for visibility to everyone.
---
Manam recap cheddam, ee meeting lo enti agreed avvalsindi?
Let's recap, what was agreed in this meeting?
---
Idi mida sign off ivvagalara andharu?
Can everyone sign off on this?
---
Ee approach ki any objections unnayaa?
Any objections to this approach?
---
Silence ga unte yes ani anukuntanu.
I'll assume yes if there's silence.
---
Naa point ni వాళ్ళకి అర్థం avvaledhu anukunta.
I think my point isn't getting across to them.
---
Ee misalignment ni resolve cheyali ముందు move avvadaniki.
We need to resolve this misalignment before moving.
---
Andhariki agreed action items pampedham.
Let's send out the agreed action items to everyone.
---
Manam ee decision ki commit avvali andharu.
We all need to commit to this decision.
---
Ee approach lo gaps unte ippude cheppandi.
If there are gaps in this approach, tell now.
---
Naaku ఒక doubt undi, clear cheddam.
I have a doubt, let's clear it.
---
Manam parallel ga different things chestunnam, align cheddam.
We're doing different things in parallel, let's align.
---
Ee meeting ki andhariki invite cheddam, andharu align avvalsina vaaru.
Let's invite everyone to this meeting, all need to be aligned.
---
Idi cross-team alignment kaavali.
This needs cross-team alignment.
---
Manam ఒక common framework ki rāvali.
We need to come to a common framework.
---
Ee thing mida explicit confirmation kaavali, assumption okay kaadu.
We need explicit confirmation on this, assumption is not okay.
---
Andhariki ee plan ki okay ena, ఒక్కొక్కరిని adugutha.
Let me ask each one if they're okay with this plan.
---
Manam align ayyamu, ippudu execution mida focus pedadam.
We're aligned, now let's focus on execution.

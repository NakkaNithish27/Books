# 290. Business cases

Manaki ee initiative ki business case rāyali.
We need to write a business case for this initiative.
---
Template undi ga, dāntho start cheddam.
There's a template, let's start with that.
---
Problem statement clear ga undali ముందు.
Problem statement needs to be clear upfront.
---
"Why" ade lead chesthe better land avtundi.
Leading with the "why" lands better.
---
Options analysis lo 3 options petti compare cheddam.
In options analysis let's put 3 options and compare.
---
Option A: do nothing, Option B: minimal, Option C: full.
Option A: do nothing, Option B: minimal, Option C: full.
---
"Do nothing" ki kuda cost undi, ade highlight cheyali.
"Do nothing" also has a cost, need to highlight that.
---
Recommended option enti, ade clearly call out cheyali.
Need to clearly call out the recommended option.
---
Financial summary upfront pettali execs ki.
Put the financial summary upfront for execs.
---
Cost ela undi, benefit ela undi — side by side.
Cost and benefit side by side.
---
3-year P&L view ivvali ee case ki.
Give a 3-year P&L view for this case.
---
Year 1 cost heavy, year 2 break-even, year 3 profit.
Year 1 cost heavy, year 2 break-even, year 3 profit.
---
Assumptions ni explicit ga list cheyali.
Explicitly list the assumptions.
---
"Assumes 20% adoption" ane caveat undali.
Need a caveat — "assumes 20% adoption".
---
Risks section lo top 5 risks call out cheddam.
Call out top 5 risks in the risks section.
---
Each risk ki mitigation kuda undali.
Each risk needs a mitigation too.
---
Dependencies clearly list cheyali — internal and external.
Clearly list dependencies — internal and external.
---
Timeline ni Gantt chart ga deck lo pettali.
Put the timeline as a Gantt chart in the deck.
---
Milestones, owners — andhi same chart lo.
Milestones, owners — all on the same chart.
---
Ask enti — funding, headcount, approval — clearly cheppali.
The ask — funding, headcount, approval — say clearly.
---
"$500K funding, 4 FTE, Q2 start" — ade ask.
"$500K funding, 4 FTE, Q2 start" — that's the ask.
---
Sponsor ఎవరు ee business case ki?
Who's the sponsor for this business case?
---
Sponsor leakapote case stuck avtundi review lo.
Without a sponsor, the case gets stuck in review.
---
Steering committee ki ee case present cheyali.
Need to present this case to the steering committee.
---
Pre-read pampedham 48 hours ముందు.
Let's send the pre-read 48 hours in advance.
---
Exec review lo 15 minutes matrame untaru.
We only have 15 minutes in the exec review.
---
Slide 1 executive summary, slide 2 ask, slide 3 financials.
Slide 1 executive summary, slide 2 ask, slide 3 financials.
---
Backup slides petti undali questions ki.
Keep backup slides for questions.
---
"Why this, why now, why us" — andhi address cheyali.
Address all of "why this, why now, why us".
---
Strategic fit ela undi org goals tho?
What's the strategic fit with org goals?
---
OKR ki tie chesthe better resonate avtundi.
Tying to an OKR resonates better.
---
Defending business case ki rehearse cheddam ముందు.
Let's rehearse defending the business case first.
---
"Em jaragdu cheyakapote" ane question ki ready ga undali.
Need to be ready for "what happens if we don't do this".
---
Cost of inaction quantify cheyali kuda.
Need to quantify the cost of inaction too.
---
ROI sensitivity show cheddam different scenarios lo.
Show ROI sensitivity in different scenarios.
---
Approval threshold ela undi finance side nundi?
What's the approval threshold from finance side?
---
$1M above ki CFO approval kavali.
Above $1M needs CFO approval.
---
Business case approved aithe next step kickoff.
If the business case is approved, next step is kickoff.
---
Approval letter mida sign undali stakeholders nundi.
Need stakeholder signatures on the approval letter.
---
Final ga business case ni close ki ఒక summary tho mugincheyali.
Finally, close the business case with a summary.
---
Post-approval, governance ela undali ade cheppali.
After approval, mention how governance will work.

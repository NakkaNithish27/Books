# 198. Organizational alignment

We feel aligned, but let's actually test whether we are.

ఈ scenario లో మన ఇద్దరి call ఏంటో చెప్పండి — అదే alignment check.
Tell me your call in this scenario — that's the alignment check.

Alignment అంటే same priority list, same sequence — రెండూ.
Alignment means the same priority list and the same sequence — both.

Pre-meeting alignment చేసుకుందాం, meeting లో debate avoid చేయడానికి.
Let's pre-align before the meeting to avoid debating in the meeting.

ఈ topic మీద VP లతో pre-wire చేస్తాను, surprises ఉండకుండా.
I'll pre-wire this with the VPs to avoid surprises.

The top-down cascade has to be clean, otherwise the translation gets lost.

ఈ message ప్రతి level లో verbatim go చేస్తోందా verify చేద్దాం.
Let's verify the message goes through verbatim at every level.

End of meeting alignment summary ఒక్క minute లో walk through చేస్తాను.
Let me walk through a quick alignment summary at the end of the meeting.

ఈ alignment doc one-pager గా write చేస్తాను, easy గా share చేయొచ్చు.
I'll write the alignment as a one-pager, easy to share around.

ఈ scenario ఎలా handle చేస్తామో — that's a real test of alignment.
How we handle this scenario is a real test of alignment.

ఈ priorities ranked గా రాద్దాం — top మీద ఏది ఉండాలో agree అయ్యే వరకు మనం aligned కాదు.
Let's write the priorities ranked — until we agree on what sits at the top, we're not aligned.

ఈ sequencing మీద difference ఉంది, that's still misalignment.
We differ on the sequencing — that's still misalignment.

Alignment అంటే agreement కాదు, "we'll move forward together" అని commit చేయడం.
Alignment isn't agreement, it's committing to move forward together.

ఈ topic మీద ఇంకా alignment లేదు, ఇప్పుడు move forward చేయొద్దు.
We're not aligned on this yet, let's not move forward right now.

ఈ peer org నుండి ఇంకా buy-in తీసుకోలేదు, ఇవ్వాళ్టి meeting పెట్టుకుందాం.
We don't have buy-in from the peer org yet, let's set up a meeting today.

ఈ FAQ doc cascade కి clarity ఇస్తుంది, write చేద్దాం.

ఈ RACI ఇప్పుడు decide చేయకపోతే, future లో finger pointing వస్తుంది.
If we don't decide the RACI now, there'll be finger pointing later.

ఈ north star మీద అందరూ aligned అని నేను assume చేస్తున్నాను, confirm చేయండి.
I'm assuming we're all aligned on the north star, please confirm.

ఈ alignment artifact ఒక్కటి ఉంటే, debates తగ్గుతాయి.

I'll document the outcome of this alignment meeting as a single decision page.

ఈ message different forums లో repeat చేయాలి, ఒక్కసారి చెప్తే alignment hold అవ్వదు.

Misalignment caught early is good news, not bad news.

ఈ alignment surface level గా ఉంది, scratch the surface and we'll find divergence.

After this alignment meeting, a unified statement will go out from leadership.

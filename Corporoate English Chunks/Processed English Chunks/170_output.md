# 170. Political positioning

ఈ topic మీద ఎవరి side తీసుకోకుండా neutral గా ఉంటా.
I'll stay neutral on this topic without taking sides.

ఈ meeting లో నేను opinion ఇవ్వను, observe చేస్తా.
I won't share opinions in this meeting, I'll just observe.

ఈ camp లో నేను identify అవ్వకూడదు openly.
I shouldn't openly identify with this camp.

ఈ leader తో alignment show చేయాలి slowly.
I need to slowly show alignment with this leader.

ఈ stance public గా తీసుకోను, private గా చెప్తా.
I won't take this stance publicly, I'll share privately.

ఈ direction leadership push చేస్తోంది, దానితో move అవ్వాలి.
Leadership is pushing this direction, I need to move with it.

ఈ initiative behind ఎవరు ఉన్నారో ముందు check చేస్తా.
I'll first check who's backing this initiative.

ఈ statement neutral గా frame చేస్తా, controversial కాకుండా.
I'll frame this statement neutrally, not controversially.

ఈ debate లో silent గా ఉంటా, both sides చూస్తా.
I'll stay silent in this debate and watch both sides.

ఈ team తో distance కొంచెం maintain చేయాలి ఇప్పటికి.
I need to keep some distance from this team for now.

Leadership change అవుతోంది, wait and watch చేస్తా.
Leadership is changing, I'll wait and watch.

ఈ shift coming గా ఉంది, నేను adapt అవ్వాలి.
A shift is coming, I need to adapt.

ఈ wave లో ride చేయాలి, against వెళ్ళకూడదు.
I need to ride this wave, not go against it.

ఈ stance తీసుకోవడంలో timing important.
Timing is important in taking this stance.

ఈ comment record లో ఉండకూడదు, casual గా చెప్తా.
This comment shouldn't be on record, I'll keep it casual.

ఈ topic sensitive, careful గా handle చేయాలి.
This topic is sensitive, needs careful handling.

ఈ political moment లో visible గా ఉండకూడదు ఎక్కువ.
I shouldn't be too visible in this political moment.

Right leader తో associate అవ్వాలి, right time లో.
I need to associate with the right leader, at the right time.

ఈ camp losing ground గా ఉంది, distance ఏర్పరచుకోవాలి.
This camp is losing ground, I need to create some distance.

ఈ rising leader behind support ఇవ్వాలి.
I should back this rising leader.

ఈ politics గుర్తు పెట్టుకొని move అవ్వాలి.
I need to move while keeping the politics in mind.

ఈ stance గురించి ముందుగానే manager తో align అవ్వాలి.
I need to align with my manager upfront on this stance.

ఈ position తీసుకోవడం risky, but worth it.
Taking this position is risky, but worth it.

ఈ moment లో loyalty show చేస్తే, పైకి వెళ్ళొచ్చు.
If I show loyalty in this moment, I can move up.

ఈ optic important, content కంటే.
The optics matter more than the content here.

ఈ comment public meeting లో avoid చేస్తా.
I'll avoid this comment in a public meeting.

ఈ alliance show off చేయకూడదు, subtle గా keep చేయాలి.
This alliance shouldn't be flaunted, keep it subtle.

ఈ political climate లో safe game ఆడాలి.
In this political climate, I should play it safe.

ఈ reorg approach అవుతోంది, position adjust చేయాలి.
A reorg is coming, I need to adjust my position.

ఈ side తీసుకుంటే long-term లో benefit.
Taking this side benefits me long-term.

ఈ topic లో నేను opinion strong గా ఉంచుకోను.
I won't hold a strong opinion on this topic.

ఈ meeting లో I read the room ముందు, తర్వాత మాట్లాడతా.
I'll read the room in this meeting before speaking up.

ఈ matter లో overcommit అవ్వకూడదు ఇంకా.
I shouldn't overcommit on this matter yet.

ఈ image build అవ్వాలి safe player గా.
I need to build an image as a safe player.

ఈ moves chess లాగే play చేయాలి, careful గా.
These moves need to be played like chess, carefully.

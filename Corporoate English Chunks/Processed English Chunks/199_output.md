# 199. Protecting teams

ఈ ask మీద directly answer ఇవ్వొద్దు, నేను first leadership తో talk చేస్తాను.
Don't respond to this ask directly, I'll talk to leadership first.

ఈ feedback team కి pass చేయొద్దు, నేను absorb చేస్తాను.
Don't pass this feedback to the team — I'll absorb it.

ఈ timeline unrealistic, leadership తో నేను push back చేస్తాను.
This timeline is unrealistic, I'll push back with leadership.

ఈ fire drill లో team ని drag చేయకుండా, నేను handle చేస్తాను.
I won't drag the team into this fire drill — I'll handle it.

ఈ commitment team తరఫున ఇవ్వొద్దు, first నేను check చేయాలి.
Don't commit on behalf of the team — let me check first.

ఈ random ping వచ్చింది, నేను filter చేస్తాను team కి వెళ్లేలోపే.
A random ping has come in, I'll filter it before it reaches the team.

ఈ peer org ask నీ priority list లో రాదు, నేను say no చేస్తాను.
This peer org's ask isn't on your priority list, I'll say no on your behalf.

ఈ weekend team disturb అవ్వకూడదు, ఏదైనా ఉంటే నాకు ping.
The team shouldn't be disturbed this weekend — if anything comes up, ping me.

ఈ no-meeting Friday block నేను enforce చేస్తాను, no exceptions.
I'll enforce the no-meeting Friday block, no exceptions.

ఈ scope creep వస్తోంది, నేను line మీద hold చేస్తాను.
There's scope creep coming in, I'll hold the line.

ఈ mistake team చేసిందని leadership కి చెప్పేటప్పుడు, నేను own చేస్తాను.
When this mistake is reported to leadership, I'll own it.

ఈ credit entirely team కి, నేను front-line లో నిలబడేది hits కోసం మాత్రమే.
The credit goes entirely to the team — I'm only out front to take the hits.

ఈ unfair criticism team మీద వచ్చింది, calibration లో నేను push back చేశాను.
There was unfair criticism of the team, I pushed back in calibration.

ఈ junior member మీద senior pressure ఎక్కువ ఉంది, నేను step in చేస్తాను.
There's too much senior pressure on this junior member — I'll step in.

ఈ "urgent" ask urgent ఏం కాదు, calm గా handle చేద్దాం.
This "urgent" ask isn't actually urgent, let's handle it calmly.

ఈ headcount cut నేను accept చేయను, business case తో defend చేస్తాను.
I'm not accepting this headcount cut, I'll defend with the business case.

ఈ exec team ని directly ping చేయొద్దు అని చెప్తాను, నేను route అవుతాను.
I'll ask the exec not to ping the team directly — route it through me.

ఈ meeting team-wide అవసరం లేదు, నేను attend చేస్తాను only.
This meeting doesn't need to be team-wide, I'll attend on behalf.

ఈ ask మీద line drawing నేను చేస్తాను, you focus on the work.
I'll do the line-drawing on this ask, you focus on the work.

ఈ team గా ఈ ask politely decline చేస్తున్నాం, with context.
As a team we're politely declining this ask, with context.

ఈ noise నుండి shield చేయడం నా job, you don't owe an answer to everyone.
Shielding the team from this noise is my job — you don't owe an answer to everyone.

ఈ topic వల్ల team focus break అవ్వొద్దు, ఇది నా lane.
This topic shouldn't break the team's focus — this one is in my lane.

ఈ leadership feedback raw గా team కి hit అయితే morale dip అవుతుంది.
If raw leadership feedback hits the team directly, morale dips.

ఈ political crossfire లో team ని పడేయొద్దు, politics నాది.
Don't drop the team into political crossfire — the politics is mine to handle.

ఈ stretch request team capacity కంటే ఎక్కువ, నేను no చెప్తాను.
This stretch request is beyond team capacity, I'll say no.

ఈ last-minute ask మీద నేను firm గా push back చేస్తాను.
I'll push back firmly on this last-minute ask.

ఈ heat take చేయడం నా job, team కి cover ఇవ్వడం part of the role.
Taking the heat is my job, covering the team is part of the role.

ఈ retro లో team blame అవ్వకూడదు, structural issue అని frame చేస్తాను.
The team shouldn't get blamed in this retro — I'll frame it as a structural issue.

ఈ team మీద leadership కి narrative నేను write చేస్తాను, hearsay కి place లేదు.
I'll write the narrative about this team to leadership — there's no room for hearsay.

ఈ ask నీకు ఎవరైనా అడిగితే, "let me check with my manager" అని reply చేయి, దాంతో end.
If anyone hits you with this ask, just reply "let me check with my manager" — that ends it there.

# 104. Negotiating trade-offs

Manam andhi do at the same time cheyalemu, oka thing pick cheyali.
We can't do all this at the same time, we have to pick one.
---
Scope or timeline - rendu lo edi compromise cheddam?
Scope or timeline — which one do we compromise on?
---
Ee timeline lo full scope possible kaadu.
Full scope isn't possible in this timeline.
---
Quality kaavala, speed kaavala - cheppandi.
You want quality or speed — tell me.
---
Rendu okesari kaalemu honestly.
Honestly, we can't have both at once.
---
Em drop cheddam? Ee feature or aa feature?
What do we drop? This feature or that one?
---
Must-have enti, nice-to-have enti - separate cheddam.
Let's separate must-haves from nice-to-haves.
---
Idi must-have aithe, deeniki compromise cheyali వేరే daggara.
If this is a must-have, we have to compromise somewhere else.
---
Ee feature drop cheste timeline meet avtundi.
If we drop this feature, we can meet the timeline.
---
Resources తక్కువ unnayi, ఏదో okati pick cheyali.
Resources are limited, we have to pick one.
---
Manaki ఇంకొకరిని add cheste timeline manage avtundi.
If we add one more person, we can manage the timeline.
---
Trade-off undi ikkada, cheppandi mee priority enti.
There's a trade-off here, tell me what your priority is.
---
Ee thing chesaaru ante aa thing aapali.
If we do this, we have to pause that.
---
Bandwidth limited, andhi cover cheyalemu.
Bandwidth is limited, we can't cover everything.
---
Ee feature ki extra week kaavali.
We need an extra week for this feature.
---
Either scope tagginchandi, leda deadline pencidi.
Either reduce the scope, or extend the deadline.
---
Rendu side balance cheyaali manam.
We need to balance both sides.
---
Ee request accommodate cheste vere thing slip avtundi.
If we accommodate this request, something else will slip.
---
Manam ఏం give up cheyadaniki ready ga unnam?
What are we ready to give up?
---
Priority list లో edi top lo undi cheppandi.
Tell me what's at the top of the priority list.
---
P0 enti, P1 enti, P2 enti - clarify cheddam.
Let's clarify what's P0, P1, P2.
---
P0 only ee sprint lo, migatha next sprint ki push cheddam.
Only P0 this sprint, the rest we'll push to next sprint.
---
Idi cheyali ante ade ఆగాలి, accept ena?
If we do this, that has to wait — okay with that?
---
Ee trade-off ki manager buy-in kaavali.
We need manager buy-in on this trade-off.
---
Idi short-term lo manage avtundi, long-term lo problem.
Short-term it'll manage, long-term it's a problem.
---
Technical debt vastundi ila chesaaru ante.
We'll accumulate technical debt if we go this way.
---
Quick fix or proper fix - rendu different efforts.
Quick fix or proper fix — both are different efforts.
---
Manam pragmatic ga undali ikkada, perfect kaadu.
We need to be pragmatic here, not perfect.
---
Eighty percent solution chālu ee sprint ki.
An 80% solution is enough for this sprint.
---
Remaining twenty percent next iteration lo cover cheddam.
We'll cover the remaining 20% in the next iteration.
---
Idi acceptable trade-off, naa opinion lo.
This is an acceptable trade-off, in my opinion.
---
Risk vs reward chudali decision teesukunemundu.
We need to look at risk vs reward before deciding.
---
Manam ee call lo decide cheyali enti negotiate cheyalo.
We need to decide in this call what to negotiate.
---
Vaaళ్ళతో push back cheddam, idi reasonable kaadu.
Let's push back with them, this isn't reasonable.
---
Idi మన ki manageable kaadu current state lo.
This isn't manageable for us in the current state.
---
Manam asks ni split cheddam, andhi okesari kaadu.
Let's split the asks, not all at once.
---
Phase 1, phase 2 ga break cheste better.
Better to break it as phase 1, phase 2.
---
Ee compromise mida andharu okay ena?
Is everyone okay with this compromise?
---
Win-win solution chudali, oka side matrame win avvakudadu.
We need a win-win solution, not just one side winning.
---
Manam meet cheddam middle lo, full ga ఎవ్వరు back off avvodhu.
Let's meet in the middle, nobody fully backs off.
---
Final ga ee trade-off accept chestam, document cheddam.
Finally we accept this trade-off, let's document it.
---
Ee decision ki revisit cheddam tarvata if needed.
We'll revisit this decision later if needed.

278. Building influence

Influence అన్నది title తో రాదు, trust తో వస్తుంది.
Influence doesn't come with a title, it comes with trust.

People నన్ను listen చేయాలంటే first respect earn చేయాలి.
For people to listen to me, I first need to earn respect.

నేను చెప్పేది value add చేయాలి.
Whatever I say should add value.

Every meeting లో మాట్లాడాల్సిన అవసరం లేదు, కానీ మాట్లాడినప్పుడు substance ఉండాలి.
I don't need to speak in every meeting, but when I do, there should be substance.

Right time లో right thing చెప్పడం important.
Saying the right thing at the right time is important.

People నన్ను go-to person గా చూడాలి ఈ topic కి.
People should see me as the go-to person for this topic.

ఒక area లో depth ఉంటే automatic గా influence వస్తుంది.
If I have depth in one area, influence comes automatically.

Loud గా ఉండడం influence కాదు.
Being loud isn't influence.

Quiet గా consistent గా deliver చేస్తే people notice చేస్తారు.
If I deliver quietly and consistently, people will notice.

Relationships build చేయడం key.
Building relationships is key.

Cross-team గా connections ఉండాలి.
I should have cross-team connections.

Just my team తో limited అవ్వకూడదు.
I shouldn't be limited to just my team.

Stakeholders తో regular గా touch in ఉండాలి.
I should stay in regular touch with stakeholders.

Casual conversations కూడా influence build చేస్తాయి.
Even casual conversations build influence.

Coffee chats underrated.
Coffee chats are underrated.

People నన్ను like చేస్తేనే listen చేస్తారు.
People will listen only if they like me.

Likeable గా ఉండడం weakness కాదు, strength.
Being likeable isn't a weakness, it's a strength.

Help చేస్తూ ఉంటే people remember చేస్తారు.
If I keep helping, people will remember.

Give first, ask later అన్నది principle.
Give first, ask later - that's the principle.

Favours track చేయొద్దు, naturally help చేయాలి.
Don't track favours, just help naturally.

Decisions లో నా input matter అవ్వాలంటే data ready గా ఉండాలి.
For my input to matter in decisions, I should have data ready.

Just opinion చెప్తే people convince అవ్వరు.
Just giving an opinion won't convince people.

Numbers, examples, evidence తో మాట్లాడాలి.
I should speak with numbers, examples, evidence.

Confidence తో మాట్లాడాలి, కానీ arrogant గా కాదు.
I should speak with confidence, but not arrogantly.

Body language కూడా influence ని impact చేస్తుంది.
Body language also impacts influence.

Eye contact, posture, tone - అన్నీ matter.
Eye contact, posture, tone - all matter.

Disagreement respectful గా express చేయాలి.
Disagreement should be expressed respectfully.

"I see it differently" అనడం better than "you are wrong".
"I see it differently" is better than "you are wrong".

People feel valued గా feel అవ్వాలి నాతో మాట్లాడేటప్పుడు.
People should feel valued when talking to me.

Listening influence build చేస్తుంది.
Listening builds influence.

People నేను listen చేస్తున్నాను అని feel అవ్వాలి.
People should feel that I'm listening.

Names గుర్తు పెట్టుకుంటే personal touch ఉంటుంది.
Remembering names adds a personal touch.

Small gestures big impact create చేస్తాయి.
Small gestures create big impact.

Credit ఇతరులకు ఇవ్వాలి, blame నేను తీసుకోవాలి.
Give credit to others, take blame on myself.

ఇది leader behavior, ఇది influence build చేస్తుంది.
This is leader behavior, this builds influence.

Senior leaders ముందు visible గా ఉండాలి.
I should be visible in front of senior leaders.

Just work చేస్తే వాళ్ళకి తెలియదు.
If I just work, they won't know.

Update emails, presentations - వీటిలో presence ఉండాలి.
Update emails, presentations - I should have presence in these.

Influence overnight build అవ్వదు.
Influence isn't built overnight.

Slowly, consistently effort పెడితే organic గా grow అవుతుంది.
With slow, consistent effort, it grows organically.

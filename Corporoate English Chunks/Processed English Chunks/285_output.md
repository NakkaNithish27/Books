# 285. Change advocacy

Ee change avasaram, naa side nundi naaki conviction undi.
This change is necessary, I have conviction from my side.
---
Manaki status quo lo undi pothe risk ekuva.
There's more risk if we stay in the status quo.
---

We need to first tell them the reason behind this change.
---

We need a strong answer to the "why now" question.
---

There's change fatigue in the team, need to roll out carefully.
---

Let's introduce this change in small chunks.
---
Big bang kāadu, phased rollout cheyali.
Not big bang, need a phased rollout.
---

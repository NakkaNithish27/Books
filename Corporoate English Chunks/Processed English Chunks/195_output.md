# 195. Building culture

Culture అంటే posters మీద ఉన్నది కాదు, daily గా మనం ఏం tolerate చేస్తామో అది.
Culture isn't what's on the posters, it's what we tolerate day to day.

ఈ behavior మనం celebrate చేస్తే, అది repeat అవుతుంది.
Whatever behavior we celebrate gets repeated.

Hire for skill, fire for fit అని rule పెట్టుకుందాం.
Let's keep it simple — hire for skill, fire for fit.

ఈ standup ritual మనకి identity, miss అవ్వకూడదు.
This standup ritual is our identity, we shouldn't miss it.

Blameless postmortem అంటే no blame, but full accountability.
Blameless postmortem means no blame, but full accountability.

Psychological safety మనం say చేయడం వేరు, build చేయడం వేరు.
Saying we have psychological safety is one thing, building it is another.

Bad behavior private గా call out చేస్తాను, good behavior public గా.
I call out bad behavior privately, good behavior publicly.

ఈ value live చేస్తున్నామా అని weekly check చేసుకోవాలి.
We need to check weekly whether we're actually living this value.

ఈ team లో low-ego ఉండాలి, no exceptions.
This team has to be low-ego, no exceptions.

Speak-up culture build అవ్వాలంటే, leader first listen చేయాలి.
A speak-up culture only happens when the leader listens first.

ఈ behavior మన team లో accepted కాదు అని clearly చెప్పాలి.
We need to clearly say this behavior isn't acceptable on this team.

Customer obsession అన్నది talk చేయడం కాదు, decisions లో reflect అవ్వాలి.
Customer obsession isn't something you talk about, it has to show up in decisions.

ఈ Friday lunches optional but encouraged, casual gathering culture కోసం.
These Friday lunches are optional but encouraged, just for the casual gathering culture.

No-meeting Friday నీ deep work day, protect it.
No-meeting Friday is your deep work day, protect it.

Async-first norm follow చేయాలి, ప్రతిదానికీ meeting అవసరం లేదు.
Let's follow an async-first norm, not everything needs a meeting.

ఈ ritual చిన్నది, but culture కి backbone.
This ritual is small, but it's the backbone of the culture.

ఈ value live చేయని team member ని exit చేయించడం also a culture decision.
Exiting a team member who doesn't live this value is also a culture decision.

Onboarding first 2 weeks లో culture absorb అవుతుంది, intentional గా design చేయాలి.
Culture is absorbed in the first two weeks of onboarding, design that intentionally.

Remote setting లో culture build అవ్వడానికి overcommunicate చేయాలి.
In a remote setting you have to overcommunicate to build culture.

Peer-to-peer recognition cheap and powerful, encourage it.
Peer-to-peer recognition is cheap and powerful — encourage it.

ఈ team లో respect తో disagree చేయొచ్చు అని safe గా feel అవ్వాలి.
On this team it should feel safe to disagree, as long as it's done with respect.

What got us here won't get us there — culture కూడా evolve అవ్వాలి.
What got us here won't get us there — even the culture needs to evolve.

ఈ moment లో leader గా నేను ఎలా behave చేస్తానో, అది culture కి signal.
How I as a leader behave in this moment is a signal to the culture.

Transparency norm ఏంటి — bad news travels faster than good news, on purpose.
Our transparency norm — bad news travels faster than good news, on purpose.

Small rituals — cleanup, lunch together — ఇవి offsites కంటే ఎక్కువ matter చేస్తాయి.
Small rituals like cleanup and lunch together matter more than the offsites.

"We don't do that here" — ఈ ఒక్క line చాలా powerful.
"We don't do that here" — that one line is incredibly powerful.

ఈ team మీద kindness non-negotiable, not a weakness.
On this team, kindness is non-negotiable, not a weakness.

Candor with kindness అనేది మన default mode.
Candor with kindness is our default mode.

Ownership culture build అవ్వాలంటే ownership కి reward పెట్టాలి.
To build an ownership culture, you have to reward ownership.

ఈ culture instinct తో protect చేయాలి, casually erode అవ్వొచ్చు.
We have to protect this culture instinctively — it can erode casually.

# 102. Expectation management

Expectations ముందే set చేయాలి, లేకపోతే తర్వాత problem అవుతుంది.
We need to set expectations upfront, otherwise it'll become a problem later.
---
Idi ఒక్క day లో అవ్వదు, realistic గా చెబుదాం.
This won't happen in a day, let's be realistic about it.
---
Vaళ్ళు ఏం expect చేస్తున్నారో ముందు clarify చేద్దాం.
Let's clarify what they're expecting first.
---
Ee timeline tight ga undi, commit avvaledhu inka.
The timeline is tight, I haven't committed yet.
---
Over-promise చేయకండి, తర్వాత deliver చేయలేము.
Don't over-promise, we can't deliver later.
---
Under-promise chesi over-deliver cheddam.
Let's under-promise and over-deliver.
---
Idi possible kaadu ee timeline lo, honest gā చెబుదాం.
This isn't possible in this timeline, let's be honest about it.
---
Vaళ్ళకి ఇది realistic kaadu అని explain చేయాలి.
We need to explain to them that this isn't realistic.
---
Ee scope lo idi cover avvaledhu.
This isn't covered in the current scope.
---
Vaళ్ళు assumption mida unnaru, alignment levu.
They're going on assumptions, there's no alignment.
---
Manam ఏం deliver చేస్తున్నాం అది clear gā చెబుదాం.
Let's clearly tell what we're delivering.
---
Idi phase 1 lo undadu, phase 2 lo చూద్దాం.
This won't be in phase 1, we'll see in phase 2.
---
Ee feature ki ఇంకా time padutundi.
This feature will take more time.
---
Vaళ్ళకి ఇప్పుడే చెప్పేయండి, last minute lo cheppoddu.
Tell them right now, don't say it at the last minute.
---
Expectations reset chesukundam ee call lo.
Let's reset expectations in this call.
---
Idi MVP scope only, full feature కాదు.
This is only MVP scope, not the full feature.
---
Vaళ్ళు perfect ni expect chestunnaru, kaani idi v1 only.
They're expecting perfect, but this is only v1.
---
Quality vs speed, రెండూ ఒకేసారి కష్టం.
Quality vs speed, both at once is tough.
---
Trade-off undi ikkada, vaaళ్ళకి అది అర్థం అవ్వాలి.
There's a trade-off here, they need to understand that.
---
Effort estimate ki buffer add chesi cheppandi.
Add a buffer to the effort estimate and tell them.
---
Aggressive timeline ki commit avvodhu.
Don't commit to an aggressive timeline.
---
Vaళ్ళకి dependencies గురించి ముందే చెప్పేయండి.
Tell them about the dependencies upfront.
---
Idi మన control lo lekapote, expectation set cheyandi appude.
If it's not in our control, set the expectation right then.
---
Vaళ్ళు inka ఏదో different ga expect chestunnaru.
They're expecting something different.
---
Misalignment vundi, ఒకసారి sync up avvali.
There's a misalignment, we need to sync up once.
---
Scope creep avvakunda chudali.
We need to watch out for scope creep.
---
Ee request ki existing timeline lo accommodate cheyalemu.
We can't accommodate this request in the existing timeline.
---
Idi additional effort, timeline impact untundi.
This is additional effort, there'll be a timeline impact.
---
Vaళ్ళకి trade-off cheppandi - either scope or timeline.
Tell them the trade-off — either scope or timeline.
---
Manam over-commit ayyamu antaru lekapote.
Otherwise they'll say we over-committed.
---
Buffer pettukoni cheppadam better.
Better to keep a buffer and tell them.
---
Idi best-case scenario, worst case lo ఇంకా time padutundi.
This is the best-case scenario, in worst case it'll take more time.
---
Vaళ్ళకి risks ni కూడా highlight cheyandi.
Highlight the risks to them as well.
---
Hundred percent guarantee ivvalemu.
We can't give a hundred percent guarantee.
---
Best effort basis lo work avtundi.
It'll work on a best-effort basis.
---
Ee deadline ki commit avvaledhu, indicative only.
Haven't committed to this deadline, it's only indicative.
---
Final number after estimation, ippudu rough estimate matrame.
Final number after estimation, this is just a rough estimate.
---
Vaళ్ళకి ee point ni clearly call out cheyandi.
Clearly call out this point to them.
---
Last minute surprises rakudadu, set expectations now.
No last-minute surprises, set expectations now.
---
Vaళ్ళు disappointed avvakudadu deliver chesinappudu.
They shouldn't be disappointed when we deliver.
---
Idi rendu varalu padutundi minimum.
This will take a minimum of two weeks.
---
Confirm chesi cheppandi, ledante commit chestunnaru ani anuktaru.
Confirm and tell, otherwise they'll think you're committing.

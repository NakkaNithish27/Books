# 169. Executive presence

Calm గా ఉండాలి, panic చూపించకూడదు.
I need to stay calm, can't show panic.

Short గా, crisp గా చెప్పాలి, రాంబ్లే అవ్వకూడదు.
Keep it short and crisp, no rambling.

ఈ point clear గా చెప్పాలి, hesitation లేకుండా.
I need to make this point clearly, without hesitation.

Tone steady గా maintain చేయాలి meeting అంతా.
I need to keep my tone steady throughout the meeting.

ఈ question కి direct గా answer ఇస్తా, around the bush వెళ్ళను.
I'll answer this question directly, won't beat around the bush.

Eye contact maintain చేయాలి, confidence project అవుతుంది.
I'll maintain eye contact, it projects confidence.

ఈ silence comfortable గా handle చేయాలి, fill చేయాలని rush అవ్వకూడదు.
I need to handle this silence comfortably, no need to rush and fill it.

Posture sit-up గా ఉండాలి camera ముందు.
I need to sit upright in front of the camera.

ఈ pushback dignified గా receive చేస్తా.
I'll receive this pushback with dignity.

Emotion control చేయాలి, react అవ్వకూడదు వెంటనే.
Need to control the emotion, not react immediately.

ఈ answer ఇవ్వడానికి కొంచెం pause తీసుకుంటా.
I'll take a brief pause before answering this.

Pause natural గా ఉండాలి, awkward గా కాదు.
The pause should feel natural, not awkward.

ఈ "umm", "ah" తగ్గించాలి speech లో.
I need to cut down on "umm" and "ah" in my speech.

ఈ point తో నేను stand by అవుతా, change చేయను.
I'll stand by this point, won't change my stance.

Disagreement respectful గా express చేస్తా.
I'll express disagreement respectfully.

ఈ meeting లో నేను thought leader గా present అవ్వాలి.
I need to come across as a thought leader in this meeting.

Voice clear గా, slow గా ఉండాలి, rush అవ్వకూడదు.
Voice should be clear and slow, no rushing.

Body language relaxed గా but alert గా ఉండాలి.
Body language should be relaxed yet alert.

ఈ tough question కి కూడా composure తో respond అవ్వాలి.
Even for tough questions, I need to respond with composure.

Senior leaders ముందు nervous అవ్వకూడదు, regular conversation గానే చూడాలి.
I shouldn't get nervous in front of senior leaders, just treat it as a regular conversation.

ఈ talking point తో grounded గా ఉండాలి.
I need to stay grounded on this talking point.

ఈ data backup తో మాట్లాడితే authority వస్తుంది.
If I speak with data backup, it carries authority.

ఈ moment లో gravitas show చేయాలి.
This moment needs some gravitas.

Loud గా కాదు, but firm గా చెప్పాలి.
Not loudly, but firmly.

Smile timely ఇవ్వాలి, but over-smile కూడదు.
Smile when timely, but don't over-smile.

ఈ slide explain చేసేటప్పుడు, audience ని scan చేయాలి.
While explaining this slide, I should scan the audience.

Filler words avoid చేయాలి, professional tone కోసం.
Avoid filler words to maintain a professional tone.

ఈ question pop up అయితే, calm గా reframe చేస్తా.
If this question pops up, I'll calmly reframe it.

Confidence project అవ్వాలి, even uncertainty ఉన్నా.
Project confidence, even when there's uncertainty.

Energy level ఈ meeting కి match అవ్వాలి.
My energy level should match this meeting.

Over-explain చేయకూడదు, less is more here.
Don't over-explain, less is more here.

ఈ "I don't know" ఒప్పుకుంటా, but follow up చేస్తా.
I'll admit "I don't know", but I'll follow up.

ఈ situation handle చేసే విధం వాళ్ళు చూస్తారు.
They're watching how I handle this situation.

Senior గానే respond అవ్వాలి, junior గా behave చేయకూడదు.
I need to respond like a senior, not behave like a junior.

ఈ moment లో presence command చేయాలి, dominate చేయకూడదు.
This moment needs me to command presence, not dominate.

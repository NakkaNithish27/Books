# 117. Political alignment

Ee call lo who matters ade ముందు chudali.
Need to see who matters in this call first.
---
Vaaru daggara nundi buy-in ముందు తీసుకుందాం.
Let's get buy-in from them first.
---
Direct ga proposal lokela vaste resistance vasthundi.
If we go straight with the proposal, there'll be resistance.
---
Pre-align cheddam key stakeholders tho.
Let's pre-align with the key stakeholders.
---
Ee point ni vaaru voice lo cheppinchali, naa voice kāadu.
This point should be voiced by them, not me.
---
Vaaru side nundi vinapadithe weight ekuva.
If it comes from their side, it carries more weight.
---
Direct ga ee mida public ga matladaledhu naaki.
I can't speak publicly on this directly.
---
Offline lo ఒక coffee chat chesi tarvata escalate cheddam.
Let's have an offline coffee chat first, then escalate.
---
Ee meeting lo silent ga undadame better ee daggara.
Better to stay silent in this meeting here.
---
Vaaru turf undi ee area lo, careful ga undali.
This is their turf, need to be careful.
---
Ee topic mida vaaru sensitive, gentle ga matladali.
They're sensitive on this topic, need to speak gently.
---
Vaaru proud chesedi ela cheppali ade chudali.
Need to see how to say it without hurting their pride.
---
Vaaru tho parallel ga work cheste tarvata clash avtundi.
If we work in parallel with them, there'll be a clash later.
---
Vaaru ki ముందే loop cheddam, lekapote politics avtundi.
Let's loop them in first, otherwise it becomes political.
---
Vaaru ki credit ivvalsindi ivvali, lekapote vaaru block chestaru.
We need to give them credit, otherwise they'll block us.
---
Ee daggara naa stance neutral ga undali.
My stance here needs to stay neutral.
---
Naaki side teesukovadam okay kāadu ee debate lo.
Taking sides isn't okay for me in this debate.
---
Naaku ee politics lo padalsina avasaram ledhu.
I don't need to get into this politics.
---
Pakshapaatam ledu ani show cheyali, both sides ki.
Need to show no bias, to both sides.
---
Ee daggara naa boss buy-in pakka ga undali.
My boss's buy-in must be solid here.
---
Manager ki ముందే update ivvali, surprise kāakunda.
Update the manager beforehand, no surprises.
---
Skip-level meeting lo idi raise cheyalanukunte, ముందు manager ki cheppali.
If you want to raise this in skip-level, tell the manager first.
---
Go-around manager karaadu ee daggara.
Don't go around the manager here.
---
Ee mida org-wide forum lo discuss cheyalemu, internal first.
We can't discuss this in an org-wide forum, internal first.
---
Ee proposal ki ఒక senior sponsor undali ముందు.
This proposal needs a senior sponsor first.
---
Sponsor leakapote idea diluted avtundi room lo.
Without a sponsor, the idea gets diluted in the room.
---
Ee meeting lo who's in the room ade matter avtundi.
Who's in the room in this meeting matters.
---
Wrong audience kalitcaledhu, message lost avtundi.
Wrong audience won't catch it, message is lost.
---
Right people ni room lo pettali ముందు.
We need to put the right people in the room first.
---
Ee point ni leadership air cover lekunda raise cheyalemu.
Can't raise this point without leadership air cover.
---
Naaki backing kavali skip nundi.
I need backing from skip-level.
---
Vaaru name drop chesthe room silent avtundi.
If we drop their name, the room will go silent.
---
Ee daggara naa visibility ki risk undi, careful ga matladali.
There's risk to my visibility here, need to speak carefully.
---
Public ga disagree cheyakudadu boss tho.
Don't disagree with the boss publicly.
---
Offline lo cheppali, public lo support ivvali.
Say it offline, support publicly.
---
"Yes, and" use cheyali, "no, but" kāadu.
Use "yes, and", not "no, but".
---
Ee thing ni naa credit ga teesukokuda, team credit antam.
Won't take this as my credit, will say it's team credit.
---
Team credit cheppadam politically smart.
Saying team credit is politically smart.
---
Naaki ee personal agenda ledhu ani show cheyali.
Need to show I have no personal agenda here.
---
Outcome focused ga matladali, person focused kāadu.
Speak outcome-focused, not person-focused.
---
Naaki naa intent ni clear ga signal cheyali.
I need to clearly signal my intent.
---
Final ga ee daggara tact undi ga win cheyalemu, prep tho.
Finally, we can't win here without tact — only with prep.

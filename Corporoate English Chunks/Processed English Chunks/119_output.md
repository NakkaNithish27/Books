# 119. Defending decisions

Ee decision ఎందుకు teesukunnanu ade explain chestha.
Let me explain why I took this decision.
---
Naa rationale ee — ade context ivvtanu.
Here's my rationale — let me give the context.
---
Aa time lo ee data tho idi best call ayindi.
At that time, with this data, this was the best call.
---
Ee decision ki andhi consider chesa, naki ee logical.
I considered everything for this decision, this seemed logical.
---
Vere options kuda chusa — vatini enduku reject chesa ade cheppali.
I looked at other options too — let me say why I rejected them.
---
Option A naaki ee reason ki suit avvaledhu.
Option A didn't suit me for this reason.
---
Option B cost ekuva, ade vadalesa.
Option B was high cost, so I dropped it.
---
Final ga ee daggara settle ayyanu reason ee.
Finally I settled here for this reason.
---
Naa daggara aa time lo ee info matrame undi.
At that time I only had this info.
---
Better info undi unte different decision teesukunte vandi.
With better info I might've taken a different decision.
---
Hindsight is 20-20, kani ade time lo idi right.
Hindsight is 20-20, but at that time this was right.
---
Decision ni revisit cheyali ane willingness ki naaki openness undi.
I'm open to revisiting the decision.
---
New info vasthe, change cheydaniki sare.
If new info comes, happy to change.
---
Ee decision pakka kāadu ani naaku telusu, kani ade best call appudu.
I know this decision isn't perfect, but it was the best call then.
---
Defensive ga avvakuda explain chesta.
I'll explain without being defensive.
---
Naaki feedback welcome, kani decision ni ela teesukunnano arthamu cheyali ముందు.
Feedback welcome, but first understand how the decision was made.
---
Mee place lo unte mee em chesedi cheppandi.
Tell me what you'd have done in my place.
---
Same constraints lo mee solution enti ade chudali.
With the same constraints, what would your solution be?
---
Idi ఒక trade-off ade conscious ga teesukunna.
This was a trade-off I consciously took.
---
Trade-off acknowledge cheyali, hide cheyakudadu.
Acknowledge the trade-off, don't hide it.
---
Aa daggara ee thing compromise ayindi, ee thing prioritize ayindi.
Here this got compromised, this got prioritized.
---
Naa accountability lo ee decision undi.
This decision is under my accountability.
---
Naa shoulders mida ee call.
This call is on my shoulders.
---
Blame ki team mida fail cheyanu, naa decision naade.
I won't push blame on the team, my decision is mine.
---
Ee daggara naaki ownership undi, lekapote credibility podthundi.
I need ownership here, otherwise credibility is lost.
---
Stakeholders ki context tho explain cheyali.
Need to explain to stakeholders with context.
---
Decision documented undi, why ade kuda undi.
The decision is documented, the why is also there.
---
DACI matrix lo decision rights naa daggara unnayi.
In the DACI matrix, decision rights were with me.
---
Ee decision ki naa boss kuda align ayindi appudu.
My boss was also aligned with this decision then.
---
Vaaru ki ee decision naachakapote em mariyu choice undi cheppandi.
If they don't like this decision, tell me what other choice there was.
---
Status quo continue cheste em jarigedi ade pic.
Look at what would've happened if we'd continued the status quo.
---
Naa decision lo gaps unte cheppandi, learn chestha.
If there are gaps in my decision, tell me, I'll learn.
---
Process improve cheyalsindi ade focus, blame kāadu.
Focus on improving the process, not blame.
---
Ee chance lo retro cheddam, ee daggara em better cheyalo ade chudali.
Let's retro this, see what we can do better here.
---
Naa decision criteria ee — visibility kosam list out chesta.
Here are my decision criteria — let me list them for visibility.
---
Optimization ee dimension mida chesa, other dimensions mida kāadu.
I optimized on this dimension, not the others.
---
Ade pakka explain cheyali — em optimize chesa, em vadalesa.
Need to explain that clearly — what I optimized, what I dropped.
---
Idi reversible decision, mistake aithe roll back cheyagalanu.
This is a reversible decision, if it's a mistake we can roll back.
---
Naaki decision lo pride ledu, learning lo pride undi.
I don't take pride in the decision, I take pride in the learning.
---
Final ga, ee decision behind logic clear ga undali aandhariki.
Finally, the logic behind this decision should be clear to everyone.
---
Q&A ki ready ga unna, openly adugutha.
Ready for Q&A, ask openly.

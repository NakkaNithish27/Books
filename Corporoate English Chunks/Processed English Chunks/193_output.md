# 193. Managing leaders above you

మీ boss ఏం care చేస్తారో ముందుగా decode చేయాలి.
First, decode what your boss actually cares about.

Exec కి update ఇస్తే, TL;DR first — full context second.
When updating an exec, TL;DR first — full context second.

Don't bring just a problem, bring the problem with two options.

పక్కకి తీసుకెళ్ళి disagree చేయి, public లో aligned గా ఉండు.
Disagree in private, align in public.

No one likes surprises, especially leadership.

Risk early surface చేయి, late lo surprise అవ్వకుండా.
Surface risks early so there's no late surprise.

ఈ decision pre-wire చేస్తాను 1:1 లో, meeting లో smooth గా land అవ్వాలి.
I'll pre-wire this decision in the 1:1 so it lands smoothly in the meeting.

మీ boss ఎలా communicate చేస్తారో, ఆ style adopt చేయి.
Adopt your boss's communication style.

If you need air cover, ask for it directly — don't just hint and leave.

With execs, brevity beats eloquence.

మీ boss calendar చూడు, ఏం priority గా ఉందో అక్కడే తెలుస్తుంది.
Look at your boss's calendar, that's where their priorities show up.

Before walking in with an ask, anticipate the first three questions.

ప్రతి meeting goal ఏంటో spell out చేయి — decision, update, లేదా discussion.

మీ boss stressed గా ఉంటే, ఇప్పుడు ask తీసుకెళ్ళొద్దు.
If your boss is stressed, don't take an ask in right now.

Find out upfront whether they prefer written or verbal.

ఈ email forward చేస్తే, summary ఒక్క line add చేయి top లో.
If you're forwarding an email, add a one-line summary at the top.

Escalation isn't about going over the boss, it's about signalling to the boss.

ఈ initiative కోసం, your sponsorship ఇస్తే help అవుతుంది.
For this initiative, your sponsorship would really help.

You also need to reinforce your boss's narrative externally.

Let's close this topic in our 1:1, not litigate it in the group meeting.

మీ boss తో trust build అవ్వాలంటే, no-surprises track record కావాలి.
To build trust with your boss, you need a no-surprises track record.

ఈ decision మీద your guidance కావాలి, ఇది my scope outside ఉంది.

ఈ topic మీద మీ opinion ఏంటి, before I commit publicly?

If you frame the ask as a yes/no question, the decision comes faster.

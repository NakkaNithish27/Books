# 108. Risk communication

Ee thing ni risk ga flag cheyali.
We need to flag this as a risk.
---
Idi ఇంకా risk kaadu, kani avvache chance undi.
This isn't a risk yet, but there's a chance it could become one.
---
Early ga heads up ivvali ee mida.
We need to give an early heads up on this.
---
High risk ga undi ee item.
This item is a high risk.
---
Naaki concern undi ee timeline mida.
I have a concern about this timeline.
---
Idi dependent on external team, risk undi.
This is dependent on an external team, there's a risk.
---
Vaaళ్ళ delay aithe మన timeline impact avtundi.
If they're delayed, our timeline gets impacted.
---
Worst case lo em jaragvchu ade visualize cheyandi.
Visualize what could happen in the worst case.
---
Likelihood high, impact kuda high.
Likelihood is high, impact is also high.
---
Ee risk ki mitigation plan enti?
What's the mitigation plan for this risk?
---
Backup plan unda ee daggara?
Do we have a backup plan here?
---
Contingency ready ga undali.
Contingency should be ready.
---
Risk register lo add cheddam ee item ni.
Let's add this item to the risk register.
---
Ee risk ki owner ఎవరు?
Who owns this risk?
---
Idi unmitigated risk, owner assign cheyali.
This is an unmitigated risk, we need to assign an owner.
---
Naa side nundi ఒక concern raise cheyali.
I want to raise a concern from my side.
---
Idi mida silent ga undakudadu, openly cheppali.
We shouldn't stay silent on this, must speak up.
---
Risk ni hide cheste ade tarvata explode avtundi.
If we hide the risk, it'll explode later.
---
Leadership ki ee risk ni call out cheyali ee week.
We need to call out this risk to leadership this week.
---
Yellow flag ga undi ippudu, red avvache scope undi.
It's a yellow flag now, has scope to go red.
---
Ee risk materialize aithe Plan B undi మన daggara.
If this risk materializes, we have a Plan B.
---
Naaki adi visible avtundi vaaru ki visible kaaledu.
I can see this but they can't see it.
---
Visibility ivvali ee risk meeda andhariki.
We need to give everyone visibility on this risk.
---
Ee risk ni weekly status lo include cheyali.
We need to include this risk in the weekly status.
---
Risk severity high, immediate action kaavali.
Risk severity is high, immediate action needed.
---
Ee daggara help kaavali, otherwise miss avvocchu.
We need help here, otherwise it could miss.
---
Manaki ee risk ni track cheyadaniki ఒక tracker undali.
We need a tracker to monitor this risk.
---
Idi proactively call out cheyadam better, late kāakunda.
Better to call this out proactively rather than late.
---
Risk konchem grey ga undi, kani flag cheyadame better.
The risk is a bit grey, but flagging is better.
---
Idi crystallize aithe ee impact undi.
If this crystallizes, this is the impact.
---
Cost overrun avtundi gaani timeline lo unnam.
There may be cost overrun but timeline-wise we're fine.
---
Quality risk undi ee timeline lo deliver chesi.
There's quality risk if we deliver on this timeline.
---
Ee risk ni accept cheddam ena, or mitigate cheddam ena?
Do we accept this risk or mitigate it?
---
Risk acceptance ki stakeholder sign off kaavali.
Risk acceptance needs stakeholder sign off.
---
Ee risk ఎక్కువ సేపు open lo undakudadu.
This risk shouldn't stay open for too long.
---
Followup date pettali, otherwise miss avtundi.
We need to set a follow-up date, otherwise it'll get missed.
---
Risk closed avvataniki ee criteria meet avvali.
For the risk to be closed, this criteria has to be met.
---
Mitigation in progress ani status pettali.
We need to mark status as mitigation in progress.
---
Ee risk ni manam ఇంకా watch chestunnam.
We're still watching this risk.
---
Realistically cheppali, ee timeline kashtam.
Realistically speaking, this timeline is tough.
---
Sugarcoat cheyakandi, ade is true ne cheppandi.
Don't sugarcoat, just say what's true.
---
Help kaavali ee blocker ni clear cheyadaniki.
We need help to clear this blocker.

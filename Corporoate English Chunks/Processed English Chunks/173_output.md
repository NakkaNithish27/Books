# 173. Competing agendas

ఈ team కి priority వేరే, మాకు వేరే.
This team's priority is different, ours is different.

ఆ team తో ఈ goal align అవ్వదు.
This goal doesn't align with that team.

ఈ ask మన roadmap లో fit అవ్వదు ఇప్పుడు.
This ask doesn't fit in our roadmap right now.

రెండు teams రెండు directions లో pull చేస్తున్నాయి.
Two teams are pulling in two directions.

ఈ trade-off discuss చేయాల్సిన అవసరం ఉంది.
We need to discuss this trade-off.

మన ask priority, వాళ్ళ ask కూడా priority, ఎవరు decide చేస్తారు?
Our ask is a priority, their ask is also a priority, who decides?

ఈ tug of war leadership level లో solve అవ్వాలి.
This tug of war needs to be solved at the leadership level.

ఈ scope conflict ఉంది, ఎవరిది main priority?
There's a scope conflict, whose is the main priority?

ఈ resource constraint, రెండూ commit అవ్వలేం.
Resource constraint, we can't commit to both.

మన deadline ఆ team తో clash అవుతోంది.
Our deadline is clashing with that team's.

ఈ point మీద alignment లేదు ఇంకా.
There's still no alignment on this point.

రెండు sides వేరే వేరే stake holders కి accountable.
Both sides are accountable to different stakeholders.

ఈ discussion అందరూ together సిట్టింగ్ లోనే resolve అవ్వాలి.
This needs to be resolved with everyone sitting together.

ఈ priority call leadership నుండి రావాలి, మనం decide చేయలేం.
This priority call has to come from leadership, we can't decide.

ఈ ask drop చేయాలి అంటే వాళ్ళు ఒప్పుకోరు.
If we say drop this ask, they won't agree.

ఈ middle ground ఏదైనా దొరుకుతుందా?
Is there any middle ground we can find?

ఈ delivery రెండు cover అవ్వడం కష్టం.
It's tough to cover both deliveries.

ఈ trade-off without leadership air cover, take చేయలేం.
We can't take this trade-off without leadership air cover.

ఈ debate లో ఎవరి voice weight ఎక్కువ check చేయాలి.
We need to check whose voice carries more weight in this debate.

ఈ ask కి say no చేస్తే, escalation వచ్చేస్తుంది.
If we say no to this ask, an escalation will come.

ఈ direction choose చేస్తే వేరే team unhappy.
If we choose this direction, the other team will be unhappy.

ఈ matter లో neutral ground కావాలి.
We need neutral ground on this matter.

ఈ scope split చేస్తే రెండూ partial గా deliver అవుతాయి.
If we split the scope, both will deliver partially.

రెండు vision align చేయడం ఇప్పుడు పెద్ద task.
Aligning the two visions is a big task right now.

ఈ matter ఎక్కువ delay అవ్వకూడదు, decision రావాలి.
This matter can't be delayed further, a decision needs to come.

ఈ ask priority queue లో ఎక్కడ ఫిట్ అవుతుంది అని చూడాలి.
We need to see where this ask fits in the priority queue.

రెండు priorities clash అయ్యాయి అని leadership కి flag చేయాలి.
We need to flag to leadership that two priorities are clashing.

ఈ ask వద్దు అని open గా చెప్పలేం, work around చేయాలి.
We can't openly say no to this ask, need to work around it.

ఈ project goals different, alignment forcefully చేయలేం.
The project goals are different, can't force alignment.

ఈ ask మన goal కి against గా ఉంది.
This ask is against our goal.

ఈ topic loop లో leadership ఉంచాలి.
We need to keep leadership in the loop on this topic.

ఈ matter solve అవ్వకపోతే delivery slip అవుతుంది.
If this matter isn't solved, the delivery will slip.

ఈ priorities reconcile చేయడం difficult, but possible.
Reconciling these priorities is difficult, but possible.

ఈ team perspective కూడా valid, మనది మాత్రమే correct కాదు.
Their team's perspective is also valid, ours isn't the only right one.

ఈ ask కి negotiated outcome కావాలి.
We need a negotiated outcome on this ask.

This was a team effort, not just me.

XYZ worked on this part, they deserve the credit.

Everyone contributed, the credit shouldn't go to just one person.

I built this piece, the ownership is mine.

This shout-out should go to the team, not an individual.

ఈ presentation లో అందరి work mention చేస్తా.
I'll mention everyone's work in this presentation.

If leadership asks who did this piece, I'll answer clearly.

I wasn't copied on this recognition mail, that's a bit awkward.

Everyone has a stake in this win, it can't be claimed by one person.

ఈ idea నాది అని proof ఉంది, chat history లో ఉంది.

It's better if the manager calls it out, then I don't have to self-promote.

Please send this feedback in writing, for the record.

This collaboration is a collective effort, not exclusive to anyone.

If we share this credit, team morale goes up.

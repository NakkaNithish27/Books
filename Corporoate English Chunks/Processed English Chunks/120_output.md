# 120. Strategic framing

Idi mida framing matter avtundi.
The framing on this matters.
---
Just feature ga cheppakanda, business value ga cheppali.
Don't say it as just a feature, say it as business value.
---
"So what" question ki answer ivvali ముందు.
Need to answer the "so what" question upfront.
---
Vaaru ki ఇది enduku matter avtundo cheppali first.
We need to first say why this matters to them.
---
Ee project ni revenue lever ga frame cheddam.
Let's frame this project as a revenue lever.
---
"Cost" ga cheppoddu, "investment" antam.
Don't call it "cost", say "investment".
---
"Problem" ga kāadu, "opportunity" ga frame cheddam.
Not as a "problem", frame it as an "opportunity".
---
Idi org-wide goal ki ela tie avtundo show cheyali.
We need to show how this ties to the org-wide goal.
---
OKR ki link cheste leadership attention vasthundi.
If we link it to OKRs, leadership attention follows.
---
Ee initiative ki business case clear ga undali.
The business case for this initiative needs to be clear.
---
"ROI ela undi" ade question ki answer ready ga undali.
"What's the ROI" — must have the answer ready.
---
Numbers ni context lo cheppali, just numbers kāadu.
Say the numbers with context, not just numbers.
---
"Last year tho compare cheste 30% pencidi" antam.
Say "this is up 30% compared to last year".
---
Big picture ni cheppali ముందు, details tarvata.
Lead with the big picture, details after.
---
Top-down structure use cheyali deck lo.
Use top-down structure in the deck.
---
Conclusion ముందు pettali, supporting points tarvata.
Lead with the conclusion, supporting points after.
---
Audience ki adapt cheyali message ni.
Need to adapt the message to the audience.
---
CFO ki cost lo cheppali, CTO ki tech lo cheppali.
Speak in cost terms to CFO, in tech terms to CTO.
---
Same message, different angle vaaru ki adaptive ga.
Same message, different angle — adapt to them.
---
Strategic narrative undali, just tactical kāadu.
Need a strategic narrative, not just tactical.
---
Ee thing 3-year vision ki ela fit avtundo cheppali.
Need to say how this fits into the 3-year vision.
---
Vaaru ki ee work ela career impact untundo show cheyali.
Show them the career impact of this work.
---
Ee daggara story-telling lo land cheste better.
Better if we land this through story-telling.
---
Customer success story ga cheppali ee proposal ni.
Tell this proposal as a customer success story.
---
Numbers tho start cheyali, story tho mugiyali.
Start with numbers, end with the story.
---
Vaaru ki ee shift ni "transformation" ga cheppali.
Frame this shift to them as a "transformation".
---
Small ask kāadu, strategic move ga cheppali.
Not as a small ask — frame it as a strategic move.
---
Tactical detail ni strategic outcome ki tie cheyali.
Tie tactical detail to a strategic outcome.
---
"Why now" ade strong ga cheppali.
Need to say "why now" strongly.
---
Urgency ni create cheyali, kani fake kāakunda.
Create urgency, but not fake.
---
Competition angle add cheste impact pencidi.
If we add the competition angle, impact grows.
---
"Vere companies already idi chestunnaru" antam.
Say "other companies are already doing this".
---
FOMO trigger avtundi leadership ki.
Triggers FOMO for leadership.
---
Defensive ga frame cheyali — "risk of not doing" ade highlight cheyali.
Frame defensively — highlight "risk of not doing".
---
"Inaction cost ee" antam clearly.
Say "this is the cost of inaction" clearly.
---
Ee daggara framing ni manager tho test cheddam ముందు.
Let's test the framing with the manager first.
---
Vaaru ki ee narrative resonate avtunda ade chudali.
See if this narrative resonates with them.
---
Words ni careful ga pick cheyali — politically charged kāakunda.
Pick words carefully — not politically charged.
---
"Pivot" antam, "failure" antam kāadu.
Say "pivot", not "failure".
---
"Iteration" antam, "rework" antam kāadu.
Say "iteration", not "rework".
---
"Learning" antam, "mistake" antam kāadu.
Say "learning", not "mistake".
---
Final ga framing decides ela land avtundi ade.
Finally, framing decides how it lands.

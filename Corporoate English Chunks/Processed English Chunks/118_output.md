# 118. Reputation management

Naa reputation ki ee thing risk avtundi.
This thing puts my reputation at risk.
---
Idi miss aithe naaki credibility hit avtundi.
If this misses, my credibility takes a hit.
---
Naaki "reliable" ane tag undali, ade priority.
I need to be tagged as "reliable", that's the priority.
---
Over commit cheste reputation damage avtundi.
If I over-commit, my reputation gets damaged.
---
Under-promise, over-deliver - ade naa style.
Under-promise, over-deliver — that's my style.
---
Quality kosam known undali manam.
We should be known for quality.
---
Vaaru naaki ee work ki count cheyali, trust mida.
They should count me for this work, based on trust.
---
Ee miss tarvata trust ni rebuild cheyali.
After this miss, I need to rebuild trust.
---
Naa mistake ni own cheyali openly.
I need to own my mistake openly.
---
Excuses ivvakuda, ownership teesukundali.
No excuses, just take ownership.
---
"I missed it, here's the plan" antam.
We'll say "I missed it, here's the plan".
---
Mistake ni acknowledge cheste credibility actually pencidi.
Acknowledging a mistake actually builds credibility.
---
Defend ga undakunda, learn ga undali.
Not defensive, just in learning mode.
---
Naa visibility ekuva avvali leadership ki ee project lo.
My visibility with leadership needs to grow in this project.
---
Right rooms lo undali, right work cheyali.
Need to be in the right rooms, doing the right work.
---
High-visibility work ni pick cheyali strategically.
Need to pick high-visibility work strategically.
---
Silent worker ga undatame paniki rāadu, voice undali.
Being a silent worker doesn't pay, need to have a voice.
---
Naa work ni share cheyali, branded ga show cheyali.
Need to share my work, show it branded.
---
"Naa team idi build chesindi" ani cheppali credit ivvataniki.
We'll say "my team built this" for credit.
---
Spotlight ni team mida pettali, leader appears.
Put the spotlight on the team — leader appears.
---
Naaki "go-to person" ga known undali ee area lo.
I need to be the "go-to person" in this area.
---
Ee skill ki naa pere undali team lo.
My name should be associated with this skill in the team.
---
Niche pick cheyali — area lo expertise.
Pick a niche — expertise in that area.
---
Naa brand statement clear ga undali — "X ki Y delivers".
My brand statement should be clear — "for X, Y delivers".
---
Ee meeting bad ga vellindi, recover cheyali.
This meeting went bad, need to recover.
---
Tarvata follow up tho repair cheyali damage.
Damage control with a follow-up after.
---
Ee tag avoid cheyali — "always late" ane label.
Need to avoid the "always late" label.
---
One bad signal poradu, multiple cheppali good ones.
One bad signal isn't enough — need multiple good ones.
---
Naa reputation slow ga build avtundi, fast ga damage avtundi.
Reputation builds slowly, damages fast.
---
Careful ga manage cheyali daily.
Need to manage it carefully, daily.
---
Email tone, meeting body language, andhi count avtundi.
Email tone, meeting body language — all of it counts.
---
Naaki naa promises ni track cheyali, miss kāakunda.
Need to track my promises, no misses.
---
Commit chesindi deliver cheste reputation auto build avtundi.
If I deliver what I commit, reputation builds automatically.
---
Vaaru naa gurinchi ela matladtaru room outside ade matter.
What they say about me outside the room matters.
---
Naa absence lo naa case ఎవరు fight cheystaru ade chudali.
Need to see who'll fight my case in my absence.
---
Allies undali ade kosam, lone wolf paniki rāadu.
Need allies for that, lone wolf doesn't work.
---
Naa naayam cheyandi, naa work ni speak cheppanivvali.
Be fair to myself, let my work speak.
---
Bragging vaste backfire avtundi, subtle ga show cheyali.
Bragging backfires, need to show it subtly.
---
"Naa team ee numbers achieve chesindi" antam, polite ga.
We'll say "my team achieved these numbers", politely.
---
Manager ki naa wins ni report cheyali regularly.
Need to regularly report my wins to the manager.
---
Self-advocacy lekapote evaru naaku promote cheyaru.
Without self-advocacy, no one will promote me.
---
Final ga reputation is long-term, daily small bricks.
Finally, reputation is long-term — small bricks daily.

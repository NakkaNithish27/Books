# 114. Escalation management

Ee thing ni manam escalate cheyali ee level lo solve avvatledhu.
We need to escalate this, can't be solved at this level.
---
Naa level lo idi resolve avvatledhu.
This isn't getting resolved at my level.
---
Manager ni loop in cheddam ee issue ki.
Let's loop in the manager on this issue.
---
Skip-level ki escalate cheyali ee thing ni.
We need to escalate this to skip-level.
---
Ee escalation ki proper context ivvali ముందు.
We need to give proper context before this escalation.
---
Just complaint kāakunda, fact based ga ivvali.
Not as just a complaint, has to be fact-based.
---
Naaki ee escalation chesedi ఇష్టం ledhu kani option ledhu.
I don't want to escalate but there's no option.
---
Last resort ga escalate chestunna.
I'm escalating as a last resort.
---
All options exhausted, ippudu escalation only.
All options are exhausted, only escalation is left.
---
Vaaru ki ఒక heads up ivvali ముందు escalate cheyadaniki.
We should give them a heads up before escalating.
---
Surprise escalation aithe relationship damage avtundi.
A surprise escalation damages the relationship.
---
"Naaki teesuke escalate chesta" ani cheppi ade chey.
Say "I'll have to escalate" first and then do it.
---
Email lo manager ni cc lo pettali ee thread ki.
We need to put the manager on cc to this thread.
---
Cc lo pedithe vaaru seriously teesukuntaru.
If we put cc, they'll take it seriously.
---
Direct ga manager ki forward cheddam ee mail.
Let's forward this mail directly to the manager.
---
Ee daggara political ga avvakunda escalate cheyali.
Need to escalate here without making it political.
---
Just facts, no blame game.
Just facts, no blame game.
---
Naa goal blame kāadu, resolution.
My goal isn't blame, it's resolution.
---
Ee escalation matter ni objective ga frame cheddam.
Let's frame this escalation matter objectively.
---
Vaaru tappu ani show cheyadam manaki vaddu, problem solve cheste chālu.
We don't need to show they're wrong, just solving the problem is enough.
---
Konchem high-level matter ee, leadership input kavali.
This is a slightly high-level matter, needs leadership input.
---
Eka rendu paragraphs lo summary ivvandi escalation lo.
Give a one-two paragraph summary in the escalation.
---
Em try chesam, em work avvaledhu cheppali.
We need to say what we tried, what didn't work.
---
Em kavali ade clearly request cheyandi.
Clearly request what's needed.
---
Vaaru intervene cheyali, support kavali manaki.
They need to intervene, we need support.
---
Ee escalation ki SLA enti?
What's the SLA for this escalation?
---
Decision kavali ee EOD by, otherwise blocker.
Need a decision by EOD today, otherwise a blocker.
---
Naaki ee escalation tarvata response raavali okka day lo.
After this escalation I need a response within a day.
---
Post-escalation, follow-up cheyali andhariki.
After the escalation, follow up with everyone.
---
Escalation closed avvataniki ఏం complete avvali cheppali.
We need to say what completes for the escalation to close.
---
Ee escalation ni team ki update cheddam.
Let's update the team on this escalation.
---
Vaaru next time escalate kāakunda ela process cheddam ade chudali.
We need to see how to process this so they don't escalate next time.
---
Customer escalation aithe extra care kavali.
If it's a customer escalation, extra care is needed.
---
Customer ki immediate response పంపాలి, internal calm down tarvata.
Send an immediate response to the customer, calm down internally later.
---
De-escalate cheddam ee daggara, tone ni soften cheyali.
Let's de-escalate here, soften the tone.
---
Vaaru ki acknowledge cheste konchem cool down avtaru.
If we acknowledge them, they'll cool down a bit.
---
"We hear you" అని ముందు cheppali.
Lead with "we hear you".
---
Action plan ivvali ఇప్పుడే, just sympathy chālu kāadu.
We need to give an action plan now, sympathy alone isn't enough.
---
Senior leader ni call ki teesuko de-escalate avvataniki.
Get a senior leader on the call to de-escalate.
---
Vaaru voice undali room lo, weight kosam.
Their voice needs to be in the room for weight.
---
Final ga ee escalation ki RCA pampedham.
Finally we'll send an RCA for this escalation.
---
Ee thing future lo repeat avvakunda ela cheddam ade plan undali.
We need a plan so this doesn't repeat in future.

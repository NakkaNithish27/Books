# 115. Handling demanding stakeholders

Vaaru ki ade asks chala vasthayi ee week.
We're getting a lot of asks from them this week.
---
Ee request unrealistic ga undi honestly.
This request is unrealistic honestly.
---
Calm ga handle cheddam, react avvakuda.
Let's handle it calmly, without reacting.
---
Vaaru pressure pedithe naaki kuda push back avvali.
If they're pressuring, I have to push back too.
---
Bandwidth ledhu ee thing ki ee week.
No bandwidth for this thing this week.
---
"Yes" anukoni late ga miss avvadam better kāadu.
Better not to say yes and miss it late.
---
Naaki commit avvali ante data kavali ముందు.
For me to commit I need data first.
---
Vaaru daily follow up chestunnaru, manage cheyali.
They're following up daily, need to manage that.
---
Status link share cheddam, repeat follow ups taggali.
Let's share a status link, repeat follow-ups will reduce.
---
Vaariki ఒక cadence pettali update ki.
We need to set a cadence for updates.
---
"Every hour adugutha" anedi okay kāadu.
"I'll ask every hour" is not okay.
---
Naaki working time kavali, interruption tāgithe deliver avtundi.
I need working time, if interruption reduces I can deliver.
---
Vaaru ki priority list iste manaki edi ముందు cheppali.
If they give a priority list, they should say which is first.
---
Andhi P0 anlemu honestly, ఒకే time lo.
We can't call everything P0 honestly, at the same time.
---
Konchem soften cheppali no ki.
Need to soften the no a bit.
---
"Idi ee week lo possible kāadu, kani next week try cheddam" antam.
Let's say "this isn't possible this week, but we'll try next week".
---
Vaaru anger ki personal ga teesukunte naaki kasht avtundi.
If I take their anger personally, it'll be tough on me.
---
Anger ade situation mida, mana mida kāadu.
The anger is at the situation, not at us.
---
Naa professional gane respond cheyali.
I need to respond just professionally.
---
"Naaku ee point arthamayindi" ani acknowledge cheddam ముందు.
Let's acknowledge first — "I understand this point".
---
Tarvata facts cheppali, em possible em kāado.
Then state the facts — what's possible, what's not.
---
Boundary set cheyali politely.
Need to set the boundary politely.
---
"Naaki off hours lo ping cheste tarvata respond chestha" antam.
We'll say "if pinged off hours, I'll respond later".
---
Weekend ki idi okay kāadu, except real emergency.
This isn't okay for weekends, except for real emergencies.
---
Vaaru emergency ani call chestaru ప్రతిదాన్ని, manaki filter kavali.
They call everything an emergency, we need a filter.
---
"Real impact enti" ani adugutha each time.
I'll ask "what's the real impact" each time.
---
Vaaru ki manaki value cheppali, every time.
We need to remind them of our value every time.
---
Always available undadame perfect kāadu, output matters.
Being always available isn't perfect, output matters.
---
Naa manager kuda visibility undali ee daggara.
My manager also needs visibility on this.
---
Vaaru direct ga manager ki cheppali in extreme cases.
In extreme cases, my manager will speak to them directly.
---
Naaki sandwich technique use cheyali — positive, no, positive.
I'll use the sandwich technique — positive, no, positive.
---
"Mee point arthamayindi, idi ee week kāadu, next week ki track lo undi" antam.
"I get your point, not this week, tracked for next week".
---
Vaaru ki options ivvali, not flat no.
Give them options, not a flat no.
---
"Either A this week, or B this week, రెండూ kāadu" antam.
"Either A this week or B this week, not both".
---
Politely firm undali, defensive kāadu.
Need to be politely firm, not defensive.
---
Emails lo ni tone watch cheddam.
Let's watch the tone in emails.
---
Written record undali, vaaru tarvata twist cheste support ki.
Written record is needed, in case they twist later.
---
Naaki ee daggara naa ground hold cheyali.
I need to hold my ground here.
---
Saying no is also a service to them.
Saying no is also a service to them.
---
Vaaru ki long-term lo respect vasthundi clarity tho.
They'll respect us long-term for our clarity.
---
Final ga relationship maintain cheyali kani boundary lo.
Finally, maintain the relationship but within the boundary.
---
Reasonable ga undali kani pushover kāadu.
Be reasonable but not a pushover.

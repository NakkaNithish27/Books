# 110. Decision influence

Naaki ఒక suggestion undi ee daggara.
I have a suggestion here.
---
Ee approach consider cheddam ena ఒకసారి?
Should we consider this approach once?
---
Naa recommendation ela undi ante...
My recommendation would be...
---
Just thinking aloud, ee option try cheddama?
Just thinking aloud, should we try this option?
---
Data ela show chestundo chudali ముందు.
Let's see what the data is showing first.
---
Numbers ee story cheptunnayi, decision adi support chestundi.
The numbers tell this story, the decision is supported by it.
---
Ee option ki pros and cons compare cheddam.
Let's compare pros and cons for this option.
---
Naaki second option lean avtundi, reason ee.
I'm leaning toward the second option, here's the reason.
---
Devil's advocate ga play chesta okasari.
Let me play devil's advocate for a moment.
---
Ee thing ni different angle nundi chuste, em telustundi?
If we look at this from a different angle, what do we see?
---
Ee approach lo hidden cost ఏమైనా unda?
Are there any hidden costs in this approach?
---
Long term lo ee decision sustainable ena?
Is this decision sustainable in the long term?
---
Customer side nundi chuste idi ela land avtundi?
From the customer side, how does this land?
---
Naa concern ఒక thing only - scalability.
My one concern is scalability.
---
Idi 2 years tarvata kuda work avvali, ade priority.
This has to work even after 2 years, that's the priority.
---
Ee precedent set chesthe future lo ela impact untundi?
If we set this precedent, what's the future impact?
---
Vere teams ela handle chesaru ee situation ni?
How did other teams handle this situation?
---
Industry standard ela undi ee daggara?
What's the industry standard here?
---
Naaki idi naachindi, ఎందుకంటే...
I like this because...
---
Ee decision ni team ki visible cheddam ముందు.
Let's make this decision visible to the team first.
---
Vaaru buy-in iste mundu velli pothu.
If they buy in, we can move forward.
---
Ee idea ni manager ki preview iddam ముందు.
Let's preview this idea with the manager first.
---
Bilateral ga matladdam vaaru daggara ముందు.
Let's talk bilaterally with them first.
---
Pre-wire cheddam ee decision ni call ki ముందే.
Let's pre-wire this decision before the call.
---
Surprise lekunda meeting ki teesukelladu ee proposal ni.
Take this proposal to the meeting without any surprises.
---
Ee key stakeholder buy-in undali ముందు.
We need this key stakeholder's buy-in first.
---
Vaaru ki visible benefit cheppali, appude vaaru on board avtaru.
We need to show them a visible benefit, only then they'll get on board.
---
Naaki ee daggara ఒక lever undi, use cheddam.
I have a lever here, let's use it.
---
Em risks unnayo highlight cheste vaaru ki seriousness తెలుస్తుంది.
If we highlight the risks, they'll see the seriousness.
---
WIIFM cheppali vaariki — what's in it for them.
We need to tell them WIIFM — what's in it for them.
---
Ee decision vaari goals ki tie avvali.
This decision should tie to their goals.
---
Vaaru ki same outcome want chestaru, just framing different.
They want the same outcome, just the framing is different.
---
Ee daggara naaku ఒక ally kavali room lo.
I need an ally in the room here.
---
Vaaru support iste ee idea easy ga land avtundi.
If they support, this idea will land easily.
---
Idi naa idea ga present cheyalsina avasaram ledhu, manaki shared win chālu.
No need to present this as my idea, a shared win is enough.
---
Naa role ee daggara facilitator, decision maker kāadu.
My role here is a facilitator, not the decision maker.
---
Question form lo adigithe better, statement kāadu.
Better to ask as a question, not a statement.
---
"Ila aithe ela undi?" ane prashna teesukurandi.
Bring up the question "What if we did it this way?"
---
Ee subtle shift naa goal ee call lo.
This subtle shift is my goal in this call.
---
Final decision vaaride, kani naa input weigh chesthe better.
The final decision is theirs, but better if my input is weighed.
---
Push cheyakandi too hard, soft ga nudge cheddam.
Don't push too hard, let's nudge softly.
---
Decision ki naa fingerprint undali kani name undakkarledu.
My fingerprint should be on the decision but my name doesn't need to be.
